    <h5>
     <a id="_10">
     </a>
     引入问题
    </h5>
    <p>
     我们在函数设计的过车体内各种, 经常要考虑对于参数的设计, 话句话说,我们需要考虑函数需要几个参数,需要什么类型的参数, 但我们并没有考虑是否需要提供参数, 如果说函数可以访问到已经定义的数据.则就不需要提供函数参数.则就不需要提供函数形参
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-c"><span class="token keyword">void</span> <span class="token function">fun</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span>

    <h5>
     <a id="_24">
     </a>
     变量作用域
    </h5>
    <p>
     概念: 变量的作用范围,也就是说变量在什么范围有效.
    </p>
    <p>
     举例:
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-c"><span class="token keyword">void</span> <span class="token function">fun</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span>
<span class="token punctuation">{<!-- --></span>
 	<span class="token keyword">int</span> a <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token number">10</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//a作用于整个fun函数,举例位置,就是从第三行到函数的}之前</span>
    <span class="token keyword">for</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token keyword">int</span> i <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token number">10</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span> i <span class="token operator">&lt;</span> a<span class="token punctuation">;</span>i<span class="token operator">++</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token comment">//i作用整个for循环,具体位置,就是从第4行到for的}之前</span>
    <span class="token punctuation">{<!-- --></span>
        a <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token number">5</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//正确,可以赋值</span>
    <span class="token punctuation">}</span> i <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token number">4</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//错误,不可以</span>
<span class="token punctuation">}</span>
</code></pre>
    <h5>
     <a id="_41">
     </a>
     变量的分类
    </h5>
    <p>
     根据变量的作用域不同,变量可以分为:
    </p>
    <ul>
     <li>
      <p>
       全局变量
      </p>
      <p>
       解释:定义在函数之外的变量,.也称之为外部变量或者全程变量
      </p>
      <p>
       作用域: 从全局变量定义处到本源文件的结束
      </p>
      <p>
       说明: 一般情况下,全局变量都是定义在所有函数的上面
      </p>
      <p>
       举例:
      </p>
      <pre><code class="prism language-c"><span class="token macro property"><span class="token directive-hash">#</span><span class="token directive keyword">include</span> <span class="token string">&lt;stdio&gt;</span></span>
<span class="token keyword">int</span> sum <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token number">10</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
<span class="token keyword">void</span> <span class="token function">fun1</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span>
<span class="token punctuation">{<!-- --></span>
    sunm <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token number">20</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
<span class="token punctuation">}</span>

<span class="token keyword">int</span> <span class="token function">main</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span>
<span class="token punctuation">{<!-- --></span>
    <span class="token function">fun1</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
    <span class="token function">printf</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token string">"%d"</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span>sum<span class="token punctuation">)</span>
<span class="token punctuation">}</span>
</code></pre>
     </li>
    </ul>
    <p>
     建议在全局变量定义时初始化,如果不初始化,系统会对全局变量初始化(初始化0)
    </p>

     <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/e552103a8d1d48d1848f433fa775204e.png"/>

     注意:
    </p>
    <p>

      就近原则
