# MAC电脑配置VSCode写JAVA

### 一、安装JDK（Homebrew安装openJDK）

#### 使用清华源安装Homebrew

[清华源安装Home-brew教程](https://mirrors.tuna.tsinghua.edu.cn/help/homebrew/)

#### Homebrew安装JDK8

```powershell
brew tap adoptopenjdk/openjdk 
brew install --cask adoptopenjdk8

```

#### 查看JDK路径

```powershell
/usr/libexec/java_home

```
