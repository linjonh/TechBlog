    <h2>
     <a id="kafka_0">
     </a>
     kafka启动步骤
    </h2>
    <p>
     先启动zookeeper，启动命令如下
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-powershell">nohup <span class="token punctuation">.</span><span class="token operator">/</span>zookeeper-server-<span class="token function">start</span><span class="token punctuation">.</span>sh <span class="token operator">/</span>home/kafka/kafka/config/zookeeper<span class="token punctuation">.</span>properties &gt; <span class="token operator">/</span>home/kafka/kafka/zookeeper<span class="token punctuation">.</span>log 2&gt;&amp;1 &amp;
</code></pre>
    <p>
     再启动kafka，启动命令如下
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-powershell">nohup <span class="token punctuation">.</span><span class="token operator">/</span>kafka-server-<span class="token function">start</span><span class="token punctuation">.</span>sh <span class="token operator">/</span>home/kafka/kafka/config/server<span class="token punctuation">.</span>properties &gt; <span class="token operator">/</span>home/kafka/kafka/kafka<span class="token punctuation">.</span>log 2&gt;&amp;1 &amp;
</code></pre>
    <h2>
     <a id="kafka__11">
     </a>
     kafka 时不时挂掉的问题
    </h2>
    <p>
     解决 kafka 时不时挂掉的问题，原因是 zookeeper.properties和server.properties文件里的配置日志路径默认是Liunx的tmp文件夹下面的 ，tmp是存临时文件的 会隔一段时间 就会清掉，所以要改成自己配置的路径，修改如下
     <br/>
     搜索 zookeeper.properties 里的
     <code>
      dataDir=/home/kafka/zookeeper
     </code>
     <br/>
     搜索 server.properties 和kafka.properties 里的
     <code>
      log.dirs=/home/kafka/kafka-logs
     </code>
     <br/>
     改成自己需要的路径
    </p>
