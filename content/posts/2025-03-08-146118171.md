    <p style="margin-left:.0001pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     在非数类大学生数学竞赛中，Stolz定理作为一种强大的工具，经常被用来解决和式数列极限的问题，也被誉为离散版的’洛必达’方法，它提供了一种简洁而有效的方法，使得原本复杂繁琐的极限计算过程变得直观明了。本文，我们将通过几个例题介绍该定理的使用方法。

    </p>
    <h2 style="margin-left:0.0001pt; margin-right:0px; text-align:justify">
     stolz定理
    </h2>
    <p>
     <span style="background-color:null">

     </span>
     <img alt="\left \{ a_n \right \}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cleft%20%5C%7B%20a_n%20%5Cright%20%5C%7D">

      </span>
      <img alt="\left \{ b_n \right \}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cleft%20%5C%7B%20b_n%20%5Cright%20%5C%7D">

    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\left \{ a_n \right \}=\infty" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cleft%20%5C%7B%20a_n%20%5Cright%20%5C%7D%3D%5Cinfty"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="background-color:null">
      若

    <hr/>
    <p>
     <span style="background-color:null">
      2.设数列
     </span>
     <img alt="\left \{ a_n \right \}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cleft%20%5C%7B%20a_n%20%5Cright%20%5C%7D"/>
     <span style="background-color:null">
      ,

     <img alt="\left \{ b_n \right \}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cleft%20%5C%7B%20b_n%20%5Cright%20%5C%7D"/>
     <span style="background-color:null">
      满足:
     </span>
     <img alt="\left \{ b_n \right \}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cleft%20%5C%7B%20b_n%20%5Cright%20%5C%7D"/>
     <span style="background-color:null">
      严格单调递减
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="background-color:null">
      且
     </span>
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\left \{ b_n \right \}=0" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cleft%20%5C%7B%20b_n%20%5Cright%20%5C%7D%3D0"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\left \{ a_n \right \}=0" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cleft%20%5C%7B%20a_n%20%5Cright%20%5C%7D%3D0"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="background-color:null">
      若
     </span>
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{a_n-a_n-1}{b_n-b_n-1}=L" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Ba_n-a_n-1%7D%7Bb_n-b_n-1%7D%3DL"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="background-color:null">
      则
     </span>
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{a_n}{b_n}=L" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Ba_n%7D%7Bb_n%7D%3DL"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     此为
     <img alt="\frac{0}{0}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7B0%7D%7B0%7D"/>
     型未定式
    </p>
    <p>
     定理看起来非常简单易懂,且该定理与洛必达公式形似。洛必达公式描述的是函数的导数的极限与原函数的极限之间的关系,该定理描述的是数列差分后的极限与原数列极限之间的关系。
    </p>
    <h2>
     例题

    <hr/>
    <p>
     1.
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to0}\frac{1+\sqrt{2}+\sqrt[3]{3}+...\sqrt[n]{n}}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto0%7D%5Cfrac%7B1&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7B2%7D&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%5B3%5D%7B3%7D&amp;plus;...%5Csqrt%5Bn%5D%7Bn%7D%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
     （
     <img alt="\frac{\infty}{\infty}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B%5Cinfty%7D"/>
     ）
    </p>
    <p>
     解:设
     <img alt="a_n=" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a_n%3D"/>
     <img alt="1+\sqrt{2}+\sqrt[3]{3}+...\sqrt[n]{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?1&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7B2%7D&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%5B3%5D%7B3%7D&amp;plus;...%5Csqrt%5Bn%5D%7Bn%7D"/>
     ,
     <img alt="b_n=n" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_n%3Dn"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="a_n=\sum_{k=1}^{n}\sqrt[k]{k}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a_n%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Csqrt%5Bk%5D%7Bk%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="a_{n-1}=\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}\sqrt[k]{k}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a_%7Bn-1%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%5Csqrt%5Bk%5D%7Bk%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="a_n-a_{n-1}=\sqrt[n]{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a_n-a_%7Bn-1%7D%3D%5Csqrt%5Bn%5D%7Bn%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="b_n-b_{n-1}=n-(n-1)=1" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_n-b_%7Bn-1%7D%3Dn-%28n-1%29%3D1"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     设
     <img alt="L=\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{a_n-a_{n-1}}{b_n-b_{n-1}}=\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\sqrt[n]{n}}{1}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?L%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Ba_n-a_%7Bn-1%7D%7D%7Bb_n-b_%7Bn-1%7D%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%5Bn%5D%7Bn%7D%7D%7B1%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     则
     <img alt="L=\lim_{n\to\infty}e^{\frac{\ln n}{n}}=e^{\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\ln n}{n}}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?L%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7De%5E%7B%5Cfrac%7B%5Cln%20n%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%3De%5E%7B%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cln%20n%7D%7Bn%7D%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="L=e^{0}=1" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?L%3De%5E%7B0%7D%3D1"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     那么,原式极限结果为1
    </p>
    <hr/>
    <p>
     2.
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{1+\sqrt{2}+\sqrt{3}+...+\sqrt{n}}{\sqrt{n+1}+\sqrt{n+2}+...\sqrt{n+n}}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B1&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7B2%7D&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7B3%7D&amp;plus;...&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn&amp;plus;1%7D&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7Bn&amp;plus;2%7D&amp;plus;...%5Csqrt%7Bn&amp;plus;n%7D%7D"/>
     （
     <img alt="\frac{\infty}{\infty}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7B%5Cinfty%7D%7B%5Cinfty%7D"/>
     ）
    </p>
    <p>
     解: 设
     <img alt="a_n=1+\sqrt{2}+\sqrt{3}+...+\sqrt{n}=\sum_{k=1}^{n}\sqrt{k}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a_n%3D1&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7B2%7D&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7B3%7D&amp;plus;...&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Csqrt%7Bk%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="a_{n-1}=\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}\sqrt{k}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a_%7Bn-1%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%5Csqrt%7Bk%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="b_n=\sqrt{n+1}+\sqrt{n+2}+...\sqrt{n+n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_n%3D%5Csqrt%7Bn&amp;plus;1%7D&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7Bn&amp;plus;2%7D&amp;plus;...%5Csqrt%7Bn&amp;plus;n%7D"/>
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      <span style="color:#fe2c24">
       (
       <img alt="b_n" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_n"/>
       每一项内第一个n与其下标一致)
      </span>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      注意,对于
      <img alt="b_n" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_n"/>
      来说,经过观察我们不难发现
      <img alt="b_{n-1}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_%7Bn-1%7D"/>
      不单单意味着原数列的前n-1项,同时我们还应该将
      <img alt="b_n" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_n"/>
      每一项内第一个n更改为n-1。即
      <img alt="b_{n-1}=\sum_{k=1}^{n-1}\sqrt{n-1+k}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_%7Bn-1%7D%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn-1%7D%5Csqrt%7Bn-1&amp;plus;k%7D"/>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <img alt="b_{n-1}=\sqrt{n-1+1}+\sqrt{n-1+2}+...+\sqrt{n-1+n-1}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_%7Bn-1%7D%3D%5Csqrt%7Bn-1&amp;plus;1%7D&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7Bn-1&amp;plus;2%7D&amp;plus;...&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7Bn-1&amp;plus;n-1%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     则
     <img alt="a_n-a_{n-1}=\sqrt{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a_n-a_%7Bn-1%7D%3D%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D"/>
     ，
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="b_n-b_{n-1}=\sqrt{2n}+\sqrt{2n-1}-\sqrt{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_n-b_%7Bn-1%7D%3D%5Csqrt%7B2n%7D&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7B2n-1%7D-%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="L=\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{a_{n-1}}{b_{n-1}}=\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\sqrt{n}}{\sqrt{2n-1}+\sqrt{2n}-\sqrt{n}}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?L%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Ba_%7Bn-1%7D%7D%7Bb_%7Bn-1%7D%7D%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D%7B%5Csqrt%7B2n-1%7D&amp;plus;%5Csqrt%7B2n%7D-%5Csqrt%7Bn%7D%7D"/>
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      利用'抓大头'思想不难得到
      <img alt="L=\frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}-1}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?L%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%5Csqrt%7B2%7D-1%7D"/>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     那么,原式极限结果=
     <img alt="\frac{1}{2\sqrt{2}-1}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B2%5Csqrt%7B2%7D-1%7D"/>
    </p>
    <hr/>
    <p>
     3.
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}n\begin{pmatrix} \sum_{k=1}^{n}\frac{1}{n+k}-ln2 \end{pmatrix}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7Dn%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D%20%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn&amp;plus;k%7D-ln2%20%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D"/>

     <img alt="\frac{0}{0}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7B0%7D%7B0%7D"/>
     ）
    </p>
    <p>
     原式=
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{\begin{pmatrix} \sum_{k=1}^{n}\frac{1}{n+k}-ln2 \end{pmatrix}}{\frac{1}{n}}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B%5Cbegin%7Bpmatrix%7D%20%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn&amp;plus;k%7D-ln2%20%5Cend%7Bpmatrix%7D%7D%7B%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     设
     <img alt="a_n=\sum_{k=1}^{n}\frac{1}{n+k}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a_n%3D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn&amp;plus;k%7D"/>
     ,
     <img alt="b_n=\frac{1}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?b_n%3D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      对于分子来说
      <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\sum _{k=1}^{n}\frac{1}{n+k}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum%20_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn&amp;plus;k%7D"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      可以变形为
      <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{1}{n}\sum_{k=1}^{n}\frac{1}{1+\frac{k}{n}}=\int_{0}^{1}\frac{1}{1+x}dx=ln2" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%5Csum_%7Bk%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1&amp;plus;%5Cfrac%7Bk%7D%7Bn%7D%7D%3D%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B1%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7B1&amp;plus;x%7Ddx%3Dln2"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      对于分母来说
      <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{1}{n}=0" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%3D0"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      因此该极限满足
      <img alt="\frac{0}{0}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7B0%7D%7B0%7D"/>
      型未定式
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     令
     <img alt="L=\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{a_{n-1}}{b_{n-1}}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?L%3D%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Ba_%7Bn-1%7D%7D%7Bb_%7Bn-1%7D%7D"/>
