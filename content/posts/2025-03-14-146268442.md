    <p>
     最下边两种查询情况需要用关键字"union all"进行连表，这里以左边满外连接为例：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="language-sql">select ......
from 表1 as a
left join 表2 as b
on a.列名 = b.列名
union all
select ......
from 表1 as a
right join 表2 as b
on a.列名 = b.列名
where a.列名 is null</code></pre>
