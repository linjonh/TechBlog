    <h3 id="配置支持单元测试工程">
     配置支持单元测试工程
    </h3>
    <p>
     在Build Variant窗口内的Test Artifact中选择“Unit Tests“
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="Paste_Image.png" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/cc721aefc1851fada9e7019f5ab83a04.webp?x-image-process=image/format,png" title=""/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="Paste_Image.png" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/bcffb03b0c546429170430dc3d6c7e8b.webp?x-image-process=image/format,png" title=""/>
    </p>
    <p>
     打开工程的build.gradle（Module:app）文件，添加JUnit4依赖，点击Gradle sync按钮。
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      build.gradle
     </strong>
    </p>
    <pre class="prettyprint"><code class="hljs rust">dependencies {
    compile fileTree(<span class="hljs-keyword">dir</span>: <span class="hljs-string">'libs'</span>, include: [<span class="hljs-string">'*.jar'</span>])
    compile <span class="hljs-string">'com.android.support:appcompat-v7:22.1.1'</span>
    testCompile <span class="hljs-string">'junit:junit:4.12'</span>
}</code></pre>
