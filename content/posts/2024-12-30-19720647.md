     <span style="word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px; color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:14px; text-align:right">
      某系统要配套做一个android客户端，来一次android开发环境快速搭建，系统Win7，具体步骤如下：
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="font-size:14px; margin:20px 0px 0px; line-height:26px; font-family:Arial; color:rgb(51,51,51)">
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      1、下载jdk：
      <a href="http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/index.html" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="text-decoration:none; color:rgb(82,102,115)" target="_blank">
       http://www.oracle.com/technetwork/java/javase/downloads/index.html
      </a>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      安装jdk，我的安装目录是C:\Java\jdk1.7.0_17和C:\Java\jre7；
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      2、下载android SDK工具包adt-bundle-windows-x86，
      <a href="http://developer.android.com/sdk/index.html" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="text-decoration:none; color:rgb(82,102,115)" target="_blank">
       http://developer.android.com/sdk/index.html
      </a>
      <wbr/>
      大概4百M，里面包含eclipse和SDK，eclipse它已经集成好了ADT。我的报名版本adt-bundle-windows-x86-20130219；解压缩adt-bundle-windows-x86包，我的路径是C:\Java\adt-bundle-windows-x86，里面包含eclipse和sdk文件夹，还有一个SDK Manager；
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      3、配置环境变量，桌面-&gt;计算机-&gt;右键属性-&gt;高级系统设置-&gt;高级-&gt;环境变量-&gt;adminstrator的用户变量(具体和自己用户名有关)，新建或者编辑一下的变量（如已经存在就追加，不存在就新建，追加时注意用;号与之前的隔开）
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      变量名：JAVA_HOME
      <br/>
      变量值：C:\Java\jdk1.7.0_17
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      变量名：ANDROID_SDK
      <br/>
      变量值：C:\Java
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      变量名：Path
      <br/>
      变量值：.;%JAVA_HOME%\bin;C:\Java\adt-bundle-windows-x86\sdk\tools;C:\Java\adt-bundle-windows-x86\sdk\platform-tools
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      变量名：CLASSPATH
      <br/>
      变量值：.;%JAVA_HOME%\lib\tools.jar;%JAVA_HOME%\lib\dt.jar;%JAVA_HOME%\lib;
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      （注意前面的.号）
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      4、测试环境变量
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      运行-&gt;cmd，输入java或者javac测试命令看成功与否；
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      5、相关的sdk更新
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      运行C:\Java\adt-bundle-windows-x86\eclipse下的eclipse
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">

     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      更新android 4.2.2 API，其他的按需更新吧！
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      <img alt="" src="https://img-my.csdn.net/uploads/201303/28/1364453787_5028.png" style="margin:0px; padding:0px; border:0px none; list-style:none; max-width:100%" title="【转】最新基于adt-bundle-windows-x86的android开发环境搭建"/>

      6、配置android模拟器
     </p>

      打开AVD管理器

      点击start，运行模拟器

      7、真机调试
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
      设置android手机为USB调试模式，步骤：menu—&gt; 设置 —&gt; 应用程序 —&gt; 开发，选择USB调试；

     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:5px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; border:0px; list-style:none; word-wrap:normal; word-break:normal; line-height:21px">
