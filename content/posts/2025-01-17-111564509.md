3.HarmonyOS与Android对比学习

3.1 DependentLayout相当于android的RelativeLayout 相对布局
  
3.2 Ability 类似activity、AbilitySlice类似fragment
  
3.3 Data模板的Ability 类似contentProvider
  
3.4 CommonEventManager、CommonEventManager 类似eventBus
  
3.5 EventHandler 类似handler
  
3.6 DirectionalLayout 类似linearlayout
