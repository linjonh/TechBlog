    <h3 style="background-color:transparent">
     httpd的安装
    </h3>
    <pre><code class="language-bash">yum -y install httpd
</code></pre>
    <h3 style="background-color:transparent">
     httpd的使用
    </h3>
    <h4>
     启动httpd
    </h4>
    <pre><code class="language-bash">systemctl enable --now httpd
</code></pre>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      使用enable --now 进行系统设置时，会将该服务设置为开机自启并且同时开启服务
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <h4>
     访问httpd
    </h4>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="295" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/4546eff0b56344c5bf183ef7faf0a19b.png" width="1272"/>
    </p>
    <h3>
     创建虚拟主机
    </h3>
    <h4>
     基于域名
    </h4>
    <p>
     在一台主机上配置两个服务server1和server2，其中服务server1的域名为monkey,服务服务server2的域名为apple
    </p>
    <table border="1" cellpadding="1" cellspacing="1" style="width:500px">
     <caption>
      资源表-1
     </caption>
     <thead>
      <tr>
       <th>

       </th>
       <th>
        域名
       </th>
      </tr>
     </thead>
     <tbody>
      <tr>
       <td>
        server1
       </td>
       <td>
        www.aaa.com
       </td>
      </tr>
      <tr>
       <td>
        server2
       </td>
       <td>
        www.bbb.com
       </td>
      </tr>
     </tbody>
    </table>
    <p>
     配置server1
    </p>
    <pre><code class="language-bash">&lt;VirtualHost *:80&gt;
DocumentRoot /var/www/monkey
ServerName www.aaa.com
&lt;Directory /var/www/monkey&gt;
AllowOverride none
Require all granted
&lt;/Directory&gt;
&lt;/VirtualHost&gt;
</code></pre>
    <p>
     配置server2
    </p>
    <pre><code class="language-bash">&lt;VirtualHost *:80&gt;
DocumentRoot /var/www/apple
ServerName www.bbb.com
&lt;Directory /var/www/apple&gt;
Require all granted
AllowOverride none
&lt;/Directory&gt;
&lt;/VirtualHost&gt;</code></pre>
