# 学习网络安全应该具备哪些专业技能

技能一

> 精通网络安全技术：包括端口、服务漏洞扫描、程序漏洞分析检测、权限管理、入侵和攻击分析追踪、网站渗透、病毒木马防范等。

技能二

> 熟悉
> [tcp/ip协议](https://link.jianshu.com?t=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.baidu.com%2Fs%3Fwd%3Dtcp%252Fip%25E5%258D%258F%25E8%25AE%25AE%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YLrHc4Pj6vmWIWn1nsrjbL0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWDkPWm1PH0vrjc1P1bLrHfzPs "tcp/ip协议")
> ，熟悉sql注入原理和手工检测、熟悉内存缓冲区溢出原理和防范措施、熟悉信息存储和传输安全、熟悉数据包结构、熟悉
> [ddos攻击](https://link.jianshu.com?t=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.baidu.com%2Fs%3Fwd%3Dddos%25E6%2594%25BB%25E5%2587%25BB%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YLrHc4Pj6vmWIWn1nsrjbL0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWDkPWm1PH0vrjc1P1bLrHfzPs "ddos攻击")
> 类型和原理有一定的ddos攻防经验，熟悉iis安全设置、熟悉ipsec、组策略等系统安全设置；

技能三

> 熟悉windows或
> [linux系统](https://link.jianshu.com?t=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.baidu.com%2Fs%3Fwd%3Dlinux%25E7%25B3%25BB%25E7%25BB%259F%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YLrHc4Pj6vmWIWn1nsrjbL0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWDkPWm1PH0vrjc1P1bLrHfzPs "linux系统")
> ，精通php/shell/perl/python/
> [c/c++](https://link.jianshu.com?t=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.baidu.com%2Fs%3Fwd%3Dc%252Fc%252B%252B%26tn%3D44039180_cpr%26fenlei%3Dmv6quAkxTZn0IZRqIHckPjm4nH00T1YLrHc4Pj6vmWIWn1nsrjbL0ZwV5Hcvrjm3rH6sPfKWUMw85HfYnjn4nH6sgvPsT6KdThsqpZwYTjCEQLGCpyw9Uz4Bmy-bIi4WUvYETgN-TLwGUv3EnWDkPWm1PH0vrjc1P1bLrHfzPs "c/c++")
> 等至少一种语言；

技能四

> 了解主流网络安全产品{如fw（firewall）、ids（入侵检测系统）、scanner（扫描仪）、audit等}的配置及使用；
