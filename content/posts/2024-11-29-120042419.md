     二、配置内核
    </h2>
    <p>

     <br/>
     <strong>
      直接搜索 FB_SSD1307
     </strong>

    <p>

    <h2>
     <a id="_20">
     </a>
     三、修改设备树文件
    </h2>
    <p>
     添加ssd1306fb-i2c节点，0x3c是i2c设备的地址
    </p>
    <p>
     在sun8i-v3s-licheepi-zero-dock.dts中添加：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-c">	ssd1306fb<span class="token operator">:</span> ssd1306fb@<span class="token number">3</span>c <span class="token punctuation">{<!-- --></span>
        compatible <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token string">"solomon,ssd1306fb-i2c"</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
        reg <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token operator">&lt;</span><span class="token number">0x3c</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
        solomon<span class="token punctuation">,</span>width <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token operator">&lt;</span><span class="token number">128</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
        solomon<span class="token punctuation">,</span>height <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token operator">&lt;</span><span class="token number">64</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
       <span class="token comment">/* reset-gpios = &lt;&amp;pio 1 0 GPIO_ACTIVE_HIGH&gt;;*/</span>  <span class="token comment">// 没有使用复位引脚 </span>
        solomon<span class="token punctuation">,</span>page<span class="token operator">-</span>offset <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token operator">&lt;</span><span class="token number">0</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
        solomon<span class="token punctuation">,</span>com<span class="token operator">-</span>invdir<span class="token punctuation">;</span>
    <span class="token punctuation">}</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>

    <p>
     <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/673017fad6cf7f507ac929ec7322026f.png">

      因为内核没有修改，这里只编译设备树

    <h3>

     2.简单操作
    </h3>
    <p>
     驱动注册后一般会挂载为/dev/fb0或者/dev/fb1设备文件。
     <br/>
     我们查看一下
     <br/>
     <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/467a23fa88b7ac759af3cad7cc4508f3.png">
      <br/>
      可以看到我们有两个fb设备，因为我有两个屏幕。
     </img>
    </p>
    <p>
     这里可以简单测试一下。
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      随机数：执行这个命令会让屏幕出现花屏现象，fb1可以改为fb0，这样可以看出两个屏幕的变化。
     </strong>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-c">cat <span class="token operator">/</span>dev<span class="token operator">/</span>urandom <span class="token operator">&gt;</span> <span class="token operator">/</span>dev<span class="token operator">/</span>fb1
</code></pre>
    <p>
     <strong>
      清屏：执行这个命令会让屏幕不显示
     </strong>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-c">cat <span class="token operator">/</span>dev<span class="token operator">/</span>zero <span class="token operator">&gt;</span> <span class="token operator">/</span>dev<span class="token operator">/</span>fb1
