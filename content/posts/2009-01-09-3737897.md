     <font color="#000000">
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       大家知道，所谓“微软模式”是指“
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       pay-per-use model
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ”
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       (
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       “按使用付费模式”
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       )
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ，一般而言，一套软件只许配一台计算装置使用，否则，属非法使用（称为“盗版”）。微软知道，打击盗版很累人。怎么办呢？
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
      </span>
     </font>
    </p>
    <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 31.5pt">
     <font color="#000000">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       2007
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       年
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       6
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       月，微软下决心转型（秘密地进行）。实际上，直到去年圣诞节前夕，微软首次对外宣布：微软申请了“
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       pay-as-you-go model
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ”（“计量付费模式”）的技术专利（
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       No. 20080319910
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ），外界一片哗然。微软说，应当有一种使用软件和访问硬件的“计量技术”（
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       to meter
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ），以便用户“按实际使用”的计量来付费，原则是多用多付费，少用少付费。比如：使用文字处理，每小时需付费
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       1
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       美元，而玩网络游戏
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       (CPU
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       很忙
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       )
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ，则每小时需付费
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       1.25
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       美元。微软的这项“模式专利”实际上是为微软的“
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       Cloud
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       计算”预备的，是一种面向未来的经营模式。
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
      </span>
     </font>
    </p>
    <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 31.5pt">
     <font color="#000000">
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       好事多磨。过了几天
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       (
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       今年元月
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       3
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       日
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       )
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ，媒体
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       (electronista
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       编辑，
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       Shirley)
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       披露，美国专利商标局（
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       PTO
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ）最终拒绝了（
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       rebuffed
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ）微软的申请，驳回申请的核心理由是：
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       too broad and too familiar
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       。这一突如其来（
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       abrupt
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ）的拒绝粉碎了微软的美梦。有人说，即便微软花钱买通
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       PTO
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       官员，批准这项专利，
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       IBM
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       也会出面反对，加以阻止，因为，
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       IBM
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       在大型计算机上早已采用这种模式。（注：微软申请“
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       pay-as-you-go
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ”模式专利被拒一事，数日之内就被全球媒体直接引用
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt">
       60,800
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 14pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       次，截至元
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       月
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt">
       9
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       日清晨）。
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt">
      </span>
     </font>
    </p>
    <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt; TEXT-INDENT: 31.5pt">
     <font color="#000000">
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       微软此举实质上等于宣告微软传统模式的终结（盒装软件模式
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt">
       has run out
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       ）。毫无疑问，无论这项“经营模式”专利是否真的成为一项“技术专利”，这项微软经营模式的转型却是
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt">
       Bill Gates
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       和
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt">
       Steve Ballmer
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'">
       企图再次创造历史的重大创举。在新的一年里面，我们祝愿微软继续大步向前。
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="FONT-SIZE: 16pt">
      </span>
     </font>
