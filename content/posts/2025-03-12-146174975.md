    <h2>
     解压工程
    </h2>
    <pre><code class="hljs">tar -xvf kasm_release_1.16.1.98d6fa.tar.gz
mv kasm_release ~
cd ~/kasm_release/</code></pre>
    <h2>
     执行安装
    </h2>
    <pre><code class="hljs">bash install.sh --offline-workspaces /media/kasm/kasm_release_workspace_images_amd64_1.16.1.98d6fa.tar.gz --offline-service /media/kasm/kasm_release_service_images_amd64_1.16.1.98d6fa.tar.gz --offline-plugin /media/kasm/kasm_release_plugin_images_amd64_1.16.1.98d6fa.tar.gz</code></pre>
