    </p>
    <pre><code>/Users/自己的用户名/Library/Containers/com.navicat.NavicatPremium/Data/Library/Application Support/PremiumSoft CyberTech/Navicat CC/Common</code></pre>
    <p>

    </p>

Settings

conn.plist
pref.plist</code></pre>
