    <h2>
     <a id="k8sStorageClass_0">
     </a>
     k8s存储类之StorageClass
    </h2>
    <p>
     ​ k8s可以通过StorageClass，动态生成一个pv供k8s的pvc使用
    </p>
    <p>
     ​ 每个StorageClass都包含字段provisioner,parameters和reclaimPolicy
    </p>
    <p>
     ​ 具体来说，StorageClass定义一下两部分：
    </p>
    <p>
     ​ 1、PV属性，比如大小、类型等
    </p>
    <p>
     ​ 2、创建PV需要使用的存储插件，如Ceph、NFS等
    </p>
    <p>
     ​
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="image-20250312110241206" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/img_convert/3010b272ae3d6c75d441408ba37db7be.png#pic_left" width="500"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     ​ 以NFS为例，需要一个 nfs-client的自动装载程序，称之为provisioner，该程序会使用已经配置好的NFS服务器自动创建持久卷，也就是自动帮我们创建PV
    </p>
    <p>
     ​ raclimPolicy：回收策略，默认删除
    </p>
    <p>
     ​ 安装nfs provisioner，用于配合存储类动态生成pv，这里以pod模式运行
    </p>
    <p>
     ​ 将nfs-subdir-external-provisioner.tar.gz上传到harbor仓库
    </p>
    <p>
     ​ 创建sa，使pod可以操作k8s
    </p>
    <pre><code>vi servieaccount.yaml
serviceaccount.yaml 
apiVersion: v1
kind: ServiceAccount
metadata:
  name: nfs-provisioner
</code></pre>
    <p>
     ​ 对sa授权
    </p>
    <pre><code>kubectl create clusterrolebinding nfs-provisioner-clusterrolebinding --clusterrole=cluster-admin --serviceaccount=default:nfs-provisioner
</code></pre>
    <p>
     ​ 安装nfs-provisioner
    </p>
    <pre><code># 创建目录
mkdir /data/nfs_pro -p
echo '/data/nfs_pro 172.16.80.131/24(rw,no_root_squash)' &gt;&gt; /etc/exports
exportfs -arv
</code></pre>
    <p>
     ​ 创建storageclass，动态供给pv
    </p>
    <pre><code>vi nfs-storageclass.yaml 
apiVersion: storage.k8s.io/v1
kind: StorageClass
metadata:
  name: nfs
provisioner: example.com/nfs
</code></pre>
    <p>
     ​ 创建pvc，绑定动态生成的pv
    </p>
    <pre><code>vi claim.yaml
kind: PersistentVolumeClaim
apiVersion: v1
metadata:
  name: test-claim1
spec:
  accessModes: ["ReadWriteMany"]
  resources:
    requests:
      storage: 10Mi
  storageClassName: nfs
</code></pre>
    <p>
     ​ 创建pod客户端
    </p>
    <pre><code>vi nfs-deployment.yaml 
kind: Deployment
apiVersion: apps/v1
metadata:
  name: nfs-provisioner
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
       app: nfs-provisioner
  replicas: 1
  strategy:
    type: Recreate
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: nfs-provisioner
    spec:
      serviceAccount: nfs-provisioner
      containers:
        - name: nfs-provisioner
          image: 172.16.80.140/nfs-subdir-external/nfs-subdir-external-provisioner:4.0.0
          imagePullPolicy: IfNotPresent
          volumeMounts:
            - name: nfs-client-root
              mountPath: /persistentvolumes
          env:
            - name: PROVISIONER_NAME
              value: example.com/nfs
            - name: NFS_SERVER
              value: 172.16.80.131
            - name: NFS_PATH
              value: /data/nfs_pro/
      volumes:
        - name: nfs-client-root
          nfs:
            server: 172.16.80.131
            path: /data/nfs_pro/
</code></pre>
