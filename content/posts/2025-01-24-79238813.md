    <h2>
     <a id="Centos7Python37_0">
     </a>
     Centos7下安装Python3.7
    </h2>
    <p>
     首先安装依赖包，centos里面是-devel，如果在ubuntu下安装则要改成-dev，依赖包缺一不可，笔者曾安装python3未成功就是因为没有安装libffi-devel
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-shell"><span class="token function">sudo</span> yum -y groupinstall <span class="token string">"Development tools"</span>
<span class="token function">sudo</span> yum -y <span class="token function">install</span> zlib-devel bzip2-devel openssl-devel ncurses-devel sqlite-devel readline-devel tk-devel gdbm-devel db4-devel libpcap-devel xz-devel libffi-devel
</code></pre>
    <p>
     然后获取python3.7的安装包
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-shell"><span class="token function">wget</span> https://www.python.org/ftp/python/3.7.0/Python-3.7.0a1.tar.xz
</code></pre>
    <p>
     解压
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-shell"><span class="token function">cd</span> ~/Download
<span class="token function">tar</span> -xvJf  Python-3.7.0a1.tar.xz
</code></pre>
    <p>
     配置python3的安装目录并安装
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-shell"><span class="token function">cd</span> Python-3.7.0a1
./configure --prefix<span class="token operator">=</span>/usr/local/bin/python3
<span class="token function">sudo</span> <span class="token function">make</span>
<span class="token function">sudo</span> <span class="token function">make</span> <span class="token function">install</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     创建软链接
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-shell"><span class="token function">ln</span> -s /usr/local/bin/python3/bin/python3 /usr/bin/python3
<span class="token function">ln</span> -s /usr/local/bin/python3/bin/pip3 /usr/bin/pip3
</code></pre>
    <p>
     最后在命令行中输入python3，能够进入python3终端即成功安装
