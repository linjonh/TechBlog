    <p style="margin-left:.0001pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     <span style="color:#000000">
      deployment扩容缩容比较简单，下面介绍两种常用方法
     </span>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="hljs">vi deploy-demo.yaml
kind: Deployment
metadata:
  name: myapp-v1
spec:
  replicas: 2
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: myapp
      version: v1
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: myapp
        version: v1
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: myapp
        image: 172.16.80.140/myapp/myapp:v1
        imagePullPolicy: IfNotPresent
        ports:
        - containerPort: 80</code></pre>
    <p style="margin-left:.0001pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     <span style="color:#000000">
      第一种方法：修改yaml中replicas的值，应用该yaml，如replicas: 3
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:.0001pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     <img alt="" height="414" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/ddba7cf903984929bf4f4d30a0c420c3.gif" width="1022"/>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:.0001pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     <span style="color:#000000">
      第二种方法：kubectl edit deploy myapp-v1，修改replicas: 4，保存查看
     </span>
    </p>
