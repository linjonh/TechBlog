    <p>
     问题：在本地安装完oracle通过plsql工具去连接数据库出现ORA-28000: the account is locked
     <br/>
     这个提示是说当前账号被锁定了，具体原因不清楚
     <br/>
     解决办法：通过cmd终端使用system/口令连接mysql
     <br/>
     <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/bc581bc831e84eeba87f97ee204023c6.png">
      <br/>
      <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/214ae21c22564696b4aee896d5449319.png"/>
     </img>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash">ALTER <span class="token environment constant">USER</span> scott ACCOUNT UNLOCK<span class="token punctuation">;</span>  <span class="token comment"># 这里的scott对应锁定的用户名 不是固定的</span>
SELECT username, account_status FROM dba_users WHERE username <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token string">'SCOTT'</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">#查看状态如果不为locked说明已经解锁，重新登录</span>
