         <h2 style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:20px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; font-size:20px; text-align:center">
          RTMP协议发送H.264编码及AAC编码的音视频，实现摄像头直播
         </h2>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          RTMP（Real Time Messaging Protocol）是专门用来传输音视频数据的流媒体协议，最初由Macromedia 公司创建，后来归Adobe公司所有，是一种私有协议，主要用来联系Flash Player和RtmpServer，如
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            FMS
           </span>
          </span>
          ,
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            Red5
           </span>
          </span>
          ,
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            crtmpserver
           </span>
          </span>
          等。RTMP协议可用于实现直播、点播应用，通过
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            FMLE（Flash Media Live Encoder）
           </span>
          </span>
          推送音视频数据至RtmpServer，可实现摄像头实时直播。不过，毕竟FMLE应用范围有限，想要把它嵌入到自己的程序中，还是要自己来实现RTMP协议的推送。本人实现了一个
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            RTMPLiveEncoder
           </span>
          </span>
          ，通过采集摄像头视频和麦克风音频，并进行H.264和AAC编码，然后发送到FMS和crtmpserver上，实现实时直播，可以通过flash player正常观看，目前效果良好，延迟时间在
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            2秒
           </span>
          </span>
          左右。本文就介绍一下RTMPLiveEncoder的主要思路和关键点，以期对需要这方面技术的朋友有所帮助。
         </p>
         <h2 style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:20px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; font-size:20px; text-align:center">
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:5px; padding-right:640px; padding-bottom:5px; padding-left:5px; word-wrap:break-word; font-size:32px; color:white; background-color:rgb(128,0,128)">
           技术分析
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          要实现
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            RTMPLiveEncoder
           </span>
          </span>
          ，需要以下四种关键技术：
         </p>
         <ul style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; list-style-type:none; list-style-position:initial">
          <li style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; list-style-type:none; list-style-position:initial">
           采集摄像头视频和麦克风音频
          </li>
          <li style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; list-style-type:none; list-style-position:initial">
           H264编码和AAC编码
          </li>
          <li style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; list-style-type:none; list-style-position:initial">
           视频和音频数据封装为可被流媒体服务器识别的可播放流
          </li>
          <li style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; list-style-type:none; list-style-position:initial">
           RTMP协议实现报文发送
          </li>
         </ul>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          其中，前两项技术在我之前的文章“采集音频和摄像头视频并实时H264编码和AAC编码”中已经介绍过了，这里就不再啰嗦了。
         </p>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          把音视频数据封装为可播放流，这个是一个难点。仔细研究一下，你会发现，RTMP Packet中封装的音视频数据流，其实和
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            FLV
           </span>
          </span>
          封装音频和视频数据的方式是相同的，所以，我们只需要按照FLV封装H264和AAC的方式，即可生成可播放流。
         </p>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          我们再看一下RTMP协议。Adobe曾经发布过一份文档《RTMP Specification》，不过wikipedia指出这份文档隐藏了很多细节，单独根据它是无法正确实现RTMP的。不过，它还是有参考意义的。其实Adobe发布之前，RTMP协议就已经被破解的差不多了，现在也已经有比较完善的实现，比如：RTMPDump，它提供的是C语言的接口，这意味着可以很方便的在其他语言中调用。
         </p>
         <h2 style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:20px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; font-size:20px; text-align:center">
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:5px; padding-right:640px; padding-bottom:5px; padding-left:5px; word-wrap:break-word; font-size:32px; color:white; background-color:rgb(128,0,128)">
           程序框架
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          与我之前写的“采集音频和摄像头视频并实时H264编码和AAC编码”这篇文章相同，采用DirectShow技术来实现音视频采集，音频编码和视频编码，在各自线程（AudioEncoderThread和VideoEncoderThread）中循环进行，RTMP的推送另起一个线程（RtmpThread）。两个编码线程实时编码音视频数据后，将数据交与Rtmp线程，由Rtmp线程循环封装为Rtmp Packet，然后发出去。
         </p>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          线程之间的数据交换，通过一个队列DataBufferQueue来实现。AudioEncoderThread和VideoEncoderThread把数据指针post到DataBufferQueue之后，立即返回，这样就可以避免因为发送Rtmp报文的而影响到编码线程的正常执行时间。
         </p>

           librtmp
          </span>
         </h2>
         <h3 style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; font-size:14px; font-weight:normal">

           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            一、编译librtmp
           </span>
          </span>
         </h3>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          下载rtmpdump的代码，你会发现，它是一个地道的linux项目，除了一个简单的Makefile，其他什么都没有。好像librtmp不依赖于系统，我们可以不用费太多功夫，把它在windows上编译。不过，librtmp依赖于openssl和zlib，我们需要首先编译好它们。
         </p>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">

          </span>

         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          a) 解压zlib压缩包

          </span>

         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">

         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          <img align="center" alt="" src="http://www.189works.com/data/attachment/portal/et2/201202/ET23977201202171039526.jpg" style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; border-top-width:0px; border-right-width:0px; border-bottom-width:0px; border-left-width:0px; border-style:initial; border-color:initial; max-width:695px"/>

         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">

         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">

          连接成功，就可以开始循环发送报文了，这里需要指定时戳和数据类型（Audio、Video、Metadata）。这里有一点需要注意的是，在调用Send之前，buf中的数据，必须是已经封装好的H264或AAC数据流，具体见下面的介绍。

         <h2 style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:20px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; font-size:20px; text-align:center">
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:5px; padding-right:480px; padding-bottom:5px; padding-left:5px; word-wrap:break-word; font-size:32px; color:white; background-color:rgb(128,0,128)">
           H264和AAC数据流
          </span>
         </h2>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          本文提到过，RTMP推送的音视频流的封装形式和FLV格式相似，由此可知，向FMS推送H264和AAC直播流，需要首先发送"
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
           AVC sequence header
          </span>
          "和"
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
           AAC sequence header
          </span>
          "，这两项数据包含的是重要的编码信息，没有它们，解码器将无法解码。
         </p>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          AVC sequence header就是
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
           AVCDecoderConfigurationRecord
          </span>
          结构，该结构在标准文档“
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            ISO-14496-15 AVC file format
           </span>
          </span>
          ”中有详细说明。
         </p>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          <img align="center" alt="" src="http://www.189works.com/data/attachment/portal/et2/201202/ET239772012021710395211.jpg" style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; border-top-width:0px; border-right-width:0px; border-bottom-width:0px; border-left-width:0px; border-style:initial; border-color:initial; max-width:695px"/>
         </p>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          AAC sequence header存放的是
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
           AudioSpecificConfig
          </span>
          结构，该结构则在“
          <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
           <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
            ISO-14496-3 Audio
           </span>
          </span>
          ”中描述。AudioSpecificConfig结构的描述非常复杂，这里我做一下简化，事先设定要将要编码的音频格式，其中，选择"AAC-LC"为音频编码，音频采样率为44100，于是AudioSpecificConfig简化为下表：
         </p>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          <img align="center" alt="" src="http://www.189works.com/data/attachment/portal/et2/201202/ET239772012021710395212.jpg" style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; word-wrap:break-word; border-top-width:0px; border-right-width:0px; border-bottom-width:0px; border-left-width:0px; border-style:initial; border-color:initial; max-width:695px"/>
         </p>
         <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; word-wrap:break-word">
          这样，AVC sequence header和AAC sequence header的内容可以基本确定了，更详细的信息，大家可以去翻阅相关文档。接下来就是封装数据内容，然后交给librtmp发送。封装格式如下所示：
