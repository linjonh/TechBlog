     vue2用vscode调试打不上断点

 <article class="baidu_pl">
  <div class="article_content clearfix" id="article_content">
   <link href="../../assets/css/kdoc_html_views-1a98987dfd.css" rel="stylesheet"/>
   <link href="../../assets/css/ck_htmledit_views-704d5b9767.css" rel="stylesheet"/>
   <div class="htmledit_views" id="content_views">
    <p>
     需要设置 sourceMapPathOverrides 首先保证
    </p>
    <p>
     安装 JavaScript Debugger (Nightly) 插件
    </p>
    <p>
     vue.config.js 开启 source-map
    </p>
    <pre><code class="hljs">configureWebpack: {
 devtool: "source-map",
},</code></pre>
    <p>
     sourceMapPathOverrides 里的路径查询
    </p>
    <p>
     在vue网站按 F12 打开开发者工具，选择 Sources
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="351" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/e06da7f50aef4a858284a9a0f1409fd9.png" width="600"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     按 Ctrl + P 输入 App.vue，可以查看路径
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="277" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/07637895c8cd4821bf8817221476e0c6.png" width="400"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     可以看到路径是 webpack://tt/src/
    </p>
    <p>
     所以设置 sourceMapPathOverrides 为
     <br/>
    </p>
    <p>
     "webpack://tt/src/*": "${workspaceFolder}/src/*"
    </p>
    <p>
     添加调试选择 chrome
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="255" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/ea6d1f992331424baca6bfdf77af411a.png" width="300"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="241" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/1767ea84eae84e488d6a71461ec9dc16.png" width="500"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     launch.json
    </p>
    <pre><code class="hljs">    "configurations": [
        {
            "type": "chrome",
            "request": "launch",
            "name": "Launch Chrome against localhost",
            "url": "http://localhost:8080",
            "webRoot": "${workspaceFolder}",
            "sourceMapPathOverrides": {
                 "webpack://项目名/src/*": "${workspaceFolder}/src/*"
            },
        }
    ]</code></pre>
    <p>
     打上断点
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="390" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/b0c69810b86d45348ac0fd197ff8452a.png" width="400"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     开启调试
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="345" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/f47e6bfb578f45bab7f479b816e395f7.png" width="320"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     点击页面按钮可以看到进入调试了
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="341" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/54ccc0e8980d45a3bf59f5fa20d32c16.png" width="340"/>
    </p>
    <p>
