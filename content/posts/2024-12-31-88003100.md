# 后端常用开源组件合集(持续更新中)

##### 1. 常用库

[awesome](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Favelino%2Fawesome-go)
- golang开源库集合

##### 2. 编码规范

[cppguide](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgoogle.github.io%2Fstyleguide%2Fcppguide.html)
- C++编码规范
  
[CodeReviewComments](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fgolang%2Fgo%2Fwiki%2FCodeReviewComments)
- go code review建议

##### 3. 敏捷开发工具

[wekan](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fwekan.github.io%2F)
-用于实践敏捷开发的看板

##### 4. 数据分析：

[matomo](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmatomo-org%2Fmatomo)
- 类似于googel分析的项目

##### 5. RPC 框架

[grpc](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgrpc.io%2F)
- googel开源的rpc框架，基于protobuf,跨语言．
  
[brpc](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fbrpc%2Fbrpc)
- 百度开源的rpc框架
  
[tars](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2FTencent%2FTars)
- 腾讯开源的rpc框架
  
[dubbo](https://link.juejin.im?target=http%3A%2F%2Fdubbo.incubator.apache.org%2F)
- 阿里开源的rpc框架，国内java圈很火．

##### 6. 分布式协调系统

[zookeeper](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fapache%2Fzookeeper)
- 简单来说就是一个java实现的强一致的kv存储系统．
  
[etcd](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fcoreos%2Fetcd)
- go版zookeeper.

##### 7. 负载均衡

[nginx](https://link.juejin.im?target=http%3A%2F%2Fnginx.org%2F)
- 四层，七层代理
  
[haproxy](https://link.juejin.im?target=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.haproxy.org%2F)
- 类似于nginx
  
[traefik](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Ftraefik.io%2F)
- go语言编写，天然支持集成k8s.
  
[kong](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgetkong.org%2F)
- 网关，基于nginx的扩展，更方便进行管理．

##### 8. 服务器运维工具

[jumpserver](https://link.juejin.im?target=http%3A%2F%2Fwww.jumpserver.org%2F)
- 服务器登录堡垒机
  
[saltstack](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fdocs.saltstack.com%2Fen%2Flatest%2F)
- 服务器批量管理工具
  
[ansible](https://link.juejin.im?target=http%3A%2F%2Fdocs.ansible.com%2F)
- 服务器批量管理工具，基于ssh,相比saltstack来说，不用安装agent.

##### 9. 存储

###### 9.1 对象存储

[minio](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fminio%2Fminio)
  
[ceph](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fceph%2Fceph)

###### 9.2 缓存

[redis](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fantirez%2Fredis)
- 支持多少数据结构，集群性扩展，高性能．

###### 9.3 NewSql 数据库

[tidb](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fpingcap%2Ftidb)
- 底层kv实现扩展，上层支持关系性数据库协议，如mysql.
  
[cockroach](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fcockroachdb%2Fcockroach)
- 类似tidb

##### 10. 容器与资源编排

[moby](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fmoby%2Fmoby)
- 容器，曾用名docker.
  
[kubernetes](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fkubernetes)
- google的容器编排

##### 11. 服务监控以及调用追踪(APM)

[prometheus](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fprometheus%2Fprometheus)
- 国外用的较多的监控系统
  
[open-falcon](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fopen-falcon)
- 国内用的较多的监控系统
  
[opencensus-go](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fcensus-instrumentation%2Fopencensus-go)
- googel新搞的
  
[opentracing](https://link.juejin.im?target=http%3A%2F%2Fopentracing.io%2F)
- 分布式调用链追踪的定义
  
[zipkin](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fzipkin.io%2F)
- opentracing的开源实现
  
[jaeger](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fjaegertracing%2Fjaeger)
- uber搞的go版本zipkin
  
[incubator-skywalking](https://link.juejin.im?target=https%3A%2F%2Fgithub.com%2Fapache%2Fincubator-skywalking)
- 集监控与追踪于一声的apache项目
