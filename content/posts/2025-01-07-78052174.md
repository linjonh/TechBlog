     <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
      环境搭建好久，今天没什么事，整理整理写一个流程，方便以后做参考
     </div>
     <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
      搭建过程参考悠悠和虫师，除一些版本的小坑，基本没有太大难度
     </div>
     <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
      <br/>
     </div>
     <br/>
     我的版本
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      python版本：2.7.14
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      selenium版本：2.53.6
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      Chromedrive版本：2.3.1
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <br/>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255); font-weight:bold">
      一.安装python
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      1.下载地址：
     </span>
     <a href="https://www.python.org/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
      <span style="color:#03884; background-color:rgb(255,255,255); text-decoration:underline">
       https://www.python.org/
      </span>
     </a>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <a href="https://www.python.org/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
      <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
       2.查看目录
      </span>
     </a>
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      ..\python\Scripts,确保有pip.exe 和easy_install.exe
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      3.将python和..\python\Scripts添加在环境变量中
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255); font-weight:bold">
      二.安装selenium
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      1.在cmd中输入pip，确保可以显示内容
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      2.在..\python\Scripts目录下输入 pip install selenium==2.53.6
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      3.显示下载过程到100%即下载完成
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      4.在cmd中以此输入
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      python
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      from selenium import webdriver
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      webdriver.Firefox
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      如果能够打开火狐浏览器，即安装成功
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      5.selenium2仅支持47以下版本的火狐浏览器，现在最新为55，需要卸载后安装47以下版本
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      已经成功驱动了火狐，开始安装chrome
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      6.cmd中输入
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      python
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      from selenium import webdriver
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      webdriver.Chrome
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     driver.get("http://www.baidu.com")
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     如果报错且没有打开网页，则是缺少驱动
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     如果打开但是显示白屏，网址处显示data，则驱动版本太低不配套
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     7
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      .谷歌60，下载2.3.1版本
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      chromedriver驱动下载地址
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <a href="http://chromedriver.storage.googleapis.com/index.html" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
      <span style="color:#03884; text-decoration:underline">
       http://chromedriver.storage.googleapis.com/index.html
      </span>
     </a>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      将exe文件放在/\python文件下，不用再配path
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      再次输入以上代码，打开网页即成功
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255); font-weight:bold">
      三.安装pycharm
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      1.选择python安装路径
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      2.破解
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      打开
     </span>
     <a href="http://idea.lanyus.com/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
      <span style="color:#03884; background-color:rgb(255,255,255); text-decoration:underline">
       http://idea.lanyus.com/
      </span>
     </a>
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      ，点击获得注册码
     </span>
    </div>
    <div style="white-space:pre-wrap; text-align:left; line-height:1.75; font-size:14px">
     <span style="color:#393939; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      help-&gt;register 将注册码复制进来，OK
     </span>
