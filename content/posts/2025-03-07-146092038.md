    <h2>
     <a id="_0">
     </a>
     问题原因
    </h2>
    <p>
     Homebrew的service库已经过时了，需要更新
    </p>
    <h2>
     <a id="_3">
     </a>
     解决方案
    </h2>
    <ol>
     <li>
      首先将homebrew更新到最新版本
     </li>
    </ol>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash">brew update
brew upgrade
</code></pre>
    <ol start="2">
     <li>
      重新链接Homebrew的services库
     </li>
    </ol>
    <p>
     针对苹果芯片运行如下命令
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">rm</span> <span class="token parameter variable">-rf</span> <span class="token variable"><span class="token variable">$(</span>brew <span class="token parameter variable">--repo</span> homebrew/services<span class="token variable">)</span></span>
brew tap homebrew/services
</code></pre>
    <p>
     intel芯片运行如下命令
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token builtin class-name">cd</span> /usr/local/Homebrew/Library/Taps/homebrew/
<span class="token function">rm</span> <span class="token parameter variable">-rf</span> homebrew-services
brew tap homebrew/services
</code></pre>
    <ol start="3">
     <li>
      重新调用 brew services xxx 即可生效
     </li>
    </ol>
