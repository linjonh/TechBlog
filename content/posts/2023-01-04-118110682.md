**方法一：try except方法：**

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
| 1  2  3  4  5  6  7 | `def` `isset(v):`  `try` `:`  `type` `(` `eval` `(v))`  `except` `:`  `return` `0`  `else` `:`  `return` `1` |

用法：

|  |
| --- |
| `if` `isset(` `'user_name'` `):`  `print` `'user_name is defined'`  `else`  `print` `'user_name is not defined'` |
