    <p>
     下载地址:
    </p>
    <p>
     <a href="https://www.enterprisedb.com/downloads/postgres-postgresql-downloads" rel="nofollow" title="EDB: Open-Source, Enterprise Postgres Database Management">
      EDB: Open-Source, Enterprise Postgres Database Management
     </a>
    </p>
    <p>
     安装图形化安装界面安装。安装完后将bin目录配置到系统环境变量
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      执行psql -h localhost -p 5432 -U postgres  密码在安装过程中设置的
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="126" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/553d875e443c47b7ba22100996a54bd3.png" width="475">
      ​
     </img>
    </p>
    <p>
     0、修改密码
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      ALTER USER sonar WITH PASSWORD '123456';
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     1、新建用户
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      CREATE ROLE sonar LOGIN PASSWORD 1234；
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     2、新建数据库
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      CREATE DATABASE mydatabase;
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="320" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/d21a472abfe1412cb5441c2982fc3649.png" width="957"/>
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      连接到特定数据库：
      <code>
       \c dbname
      </code>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="44" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/63e6db41de7c49e29c54d78f84e779a9.png" width="475"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     3、新建表
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      CREATE TABLE users (
      <br/>
      id SERIAL PRIMARY KEY,
      <br/>
      name VARCHAR(100),
      <br/>
      email VARCHAR(100)
      <br/>
      );
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="238" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/de701d75c9b545628de416eb37be0766.png" width="433"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     4、授权
    </p>
    <p>
     数据库授权
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      grant connect on database mydatabase to sonar;
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="38" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/daf64c750a8747dbbd346fa62e6d941a.png" width="492"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     表授权
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      GRANT SELECT, INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE ON TABLE your_table TO username;
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     查询数据库
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      列出所有数据库：
      <code>
       \l
      </code>
      或
      <code>
       \list
      </code>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     查询用户
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      select rolname from pg_roles;
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="226" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/413af09849324ab689c037b138c60d68.png" width="387"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     查询表
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      列出当前数据库的所有表：
      <code>
       \dt
      </code>
      或
      <code>
       SELECT * FROM pg_catalog.pg_tables WHERE schemaname != 'pg_catalog' AND schemaname != 'information_schema';
      </code>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     删除表
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      DROP TABLE [IF EXISTS] table_name;
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     删除数据库
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      DROP DATABASE IF EXISTS mydatabase;
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     退出
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      退出psql：
      <code>
       \q
      </code>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
