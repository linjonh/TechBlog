    <p style="margin-left:.0001pt; margin-right:0; text-align:left">

      <span style="color:#000000">
       定积分定义求解数列极限是一种将数列极限问题转化为定积分问题进行求解的方法。这种方法通常适用于那些和式数列极限,其主要思路是将数列的项看作是某个函数在某一点或某一段区间上的取值或某种形式的和,然后利用定积分的性质和计算方法,来求解这类数列的极限。

    <h2 style="margin-left:0.0001pt; margin-right:0px; text-align:left">
     <span style="color:#000000">
      定积分定义
     </span>
    </h2>
    <p class="img-center">
     <img alt="" height="355" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/4a14a752e27a41f2a2ad732a8916bdd8.png" width="474"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      设函数
      <img alt="y=f(x)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?y%3Df%28x%29">
       在区间
       <img alt="[a,b]" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Ba%2Cb%5D">
        上有界,在
        <img alt="[a,b]" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Ba%2Cb%5D">
         中任意插入若干个分点
        </img>
       </img>
      </img>
     </span>
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      <span style="color:#000000">
       ​​​
       <img alt="a=x_0&lt;x_1&lt;x2&lt;...&lt;x_{n-1}&lt;x_{n}=b" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a%3Dx_0%3Cx_1%3Cx2%3C...%3Cx_%7Bn-1%7D%3Cx_%7Bn%7D%3Db"/>
      </span>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      <span style="color:#000000">
       把区间
       <img alt="[a,b]" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Ba%2Cb%5D">
        分成n个小区间
        <img alt="[x_0,x_1],[x_1,x_2]...[x_{n-1},x_n]" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Bx_0%2Cx_1%5D%2C%5Bx_1%2Cx_2%5D...%5Bx_%7Bn-1%7D%2Cx_n%5D"/>
       </img>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p>
      <span style="color:#000000">
       那么各个小区间的长度为​​​​​​：​​​
       <img alt="\bigtriangleup x1=x_1-x_0,\bigtriangleup x2=x_2-x_1...\bigtriangleup x_n=x_n-x_{n-1}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cbigtriangleup%20x1%3Dx_1-x_0%2C%5Cbigtriangleup%20x2%3Dx_2-x_1...%5Cbigtriangleup%20x_n%3Dx_n-x_%7Bn-1%7D"/>
      </span>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      接着,在每个小区间
      <img alt="[x_{i-1},x_i]" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Bx_%7Bi-1%7D%2Cx_i%5D"/>
      上任取一点
      <img alt="\varepsilon_i" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cvarepsilon_i"/>
      ,作函数值
      <img alt="f(\varepsilon_i)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?f%28%5Cvarepsilon_i%29"/>
      与小区间长度
      <img alt="\bigtriangleup x_i" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cbigtriangleup%20x_i"/>
      的乘积
      <img alt="f(\varepsilon_i)\bigtriangleup x_i" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?f%28%5Cvarepsilon_i%29%5Cbigtriangleup%20x_i"/>
      (i=1,2,…·,n),并作出和式
      <img alt="S_n=\sum_{i=1}^{n}f(\varepsilon_i)\bigtriangleup x_i" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?S_n%3D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df%28%5Cvarepsilon_i%29%5Cbigtriangleup%20x_i"/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      记
      <img alt="\lambda=max \begin{Bmatrix}\bigtriangleup x_1,\bigtriangleup x_1,...\bigtriangleup x_n\end{Bmatrix}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clambda%3Dmax%20%5Cbegin%7BBmatrix%7D%5Cbigtriangleup%20x_1%2C%5Cbigtriangleup%20x_1%2C...%5Cbigtriangleup%20x_n%5Cend%7BBmatrix%7D"/>
      ,如果不论对区间
      <img alt="[a,b]" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Ba%2Cb%5D"/>
      怎样划分,也不论在小区间
      <img alt="[x_{i-1},x_i]" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Bx_%7Bi-1%7D%2Cx_i%5D"/>
      上点
      <img alt="\varepsilon" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cvarepsilon"/>
      怎样选取,只要当
      <img alt="\lambda\rightarrow0" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clambda%5Crightarrow0"/>
      时,和式
      <img alt="S_n" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?S_n"/>
      总趋于确定的极限
      <img alt="I" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?I"/>
      ,那么称这个极限
      <img alt="I" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?I"/>
      为函数
      <img alt="f(x)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?f%28x%29"/>
      在区间
      <img alt="[a,b]" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Ba%2Cb%5D"/>
      上的定积分,记作：
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      <img alt="\lim_{\lambda\to0}S_n=\lim_{\lambda\to0}\sum_{i=1}^{n}f(\varepsilon_i)\bigtriangleup x_i=\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7B%5Clambda%5Cto0%7DS_n%3D%5Clim_%7B%5Clambda%5Cto0%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df%28%5Cvarepsilon_i%29%5Cbigtriangleup%20x_i%3D%5Cint_%7Ba%7D%5E%7Bb%7Df%28x%29dx"/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cint_%7Ba%7D%5E%7Bb%7Df%28x%29dx"/>
    </p>
    <h2>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      数列极限与定积分定义的转换
     </span>
    </h2>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      对于定积分的定义,我们可以通过将区间进行特殊划分，进而把数列极限转化为定积分来计算。例如，将区间
      <img alt="[a,b]" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Ba%2Cb%5D"/>
      进行等分，即
      <img alt="\bigtriangleup x=\frac{b-a}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cbigtriangleup%20x%3D%5Cfrac%7Bb-a%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      那么任意一个切分点
      <img alt="x_i=a+i\bigtriangleup x" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?x_i%3Da&amp;plus;i%5Cbigtriangleup%20x"/>
      ,
      <img alt="x_{i}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?x_%7Bi%7D"/>
      为
     </span>
     <span style="color:#fe2c24">
      <strong>
       等差数列,首项为a,末项为b,公差d=
       <img alt="\bigtriangleup x" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cbigtriangleup%20x"/>
      </strong>
     </span>
     <span style="color:#0d0016">
      <strong>
       ，
      </strong>
     </span>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      根据前边定积分的定义，我们需要任取一点
      <img alt="\varepsilon_i" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cvarepsilon_i"/>
      ，这里不妨令他为
      <img alt="x_i" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?x_i"/>
      ,那么我们便有：
     </span>
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      <span style="color:#000000">
       <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{b-a}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}f(a+\frac{(b-a)i}{n})=\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bb-a%7D%7Bn%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df%28a&amp;plus;%5Cfrac%7B%28b-a%29i%7D%7Bn%7D%29%3D%5Cint_%7Ba%7D%5E%7Bb%7Df%28x%29dx"/>
       （1）
      </span>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      这样就把数列极限的计算转化为求一个函数在指定区间上的定积分了。
     </span>
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      <span style="color:#000000">
      </span>
      <span style="color:#fe2c24">
       注意：
      </span>
      <span style="color:#000000">
       <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{b-a}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}f(a+\frac{(i-m)(b-a)}{n})=\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7Bb-a%7D%7Bn%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df%28a&amp;plus;%5Cfrac%7B%28i-m%29%28b-a%29%7D%7Bn%7D%29%3D%5Cint_%7Ba%7D%5E%7Bb%7Df%28x%29dx"/>
       (2)
      </span>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#000000">
      当
      <img alt="0\leq m\leq 1" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?0%5Cleq%20m%5Cleq%201"/>
      时（1）(2)两式是等价的,实际上(1)式完全可以看做(2)式m=0的情况,这里我建议大家重点记忆(2)式。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      <span style="color:#000000">
       原因：
      </span>
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="1050" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/26d2ed69b72740be93938438f4db308b.jpeg" width="1406"/>
    </p>
    <h3>
     定积分定义的一些变型式
    </h3>
    <p>
     特别地,对于(1)式来说,还有一些常见的变型式：
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\sum_{i=1}^{kn}f(a+\frac{(b-a)i}{kn})\frac{b-a}{kn}=\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bkn%7Df%28a&amp;plus;%5Cfrac%7B%28b-a%29i%7D%7Bkn%7D%29%5Cfrac%7Bb-a%7D%7Bkn%7D%3D%5Cint_%7Ba%7D%5E%7Bb%7Df%28x%29dx"/>
      ,
      <img alt="k\in N^{+}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?k%5Cin%20N%5E%7B&amp;plus;%7D"/>
      (3)
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     (3)式是最一般和通用的式子,我们务必要牢记。
    </p>
    <p>
     当我们将(1)式中的f内部以及外部的k去掉后
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\sum_{i=1}^{kn}f(a+\frac{(b-a)i}{n})\frac{b-a}{n}=\int_{ka}^{kb}f(x)dx" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bkn%7Df%28a&amp;plus;%5Cfrac%7B%28b-a%29i%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5Cfrac%7Bb-a%7D%7Bn%7D%3D%5Cint_%7Bka%7D%5E%7Bkb%7Df%28x%29dx"/>
      (4)
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     (4)式的证明很简单,其实就是整体代换的思路，此时我们将k(b-a)看做整体:
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\sum_{i=1}^{kn}f(a+\frac{k(b-a)i}{kn})\frac{k(b-a)}{kn}=\int_{ka}^{kb}f(x)dx" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bkn%7Df%28a&amp;plus;%5Cfrac%7Bk%28b-a%29i%7D%7Bkn%7D%29%5Cfrac%7Bk%28b-a%29%7D%7Bkn%7D%3D%5Cint_%7Bka%7D%5E%7Bkb%7Df%28x%29dx"/>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     当k=1,a=0,b=1时,我们便得到了利用定积分定义求解数列极限中的
     <span style="color:#fe2c24">
      ‘常客’
     </span>
     ：
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{n}f(\frac{i}{n})=\int_{0}^{1}f(x)dx" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bn%7Df%28%5Cfrac%7Bi%7D%7Bn%7D%29%3D%5Cint_%7B0%7D%5E%7B1%7Df%28x%29dx"/>
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <h3>
     转换方法
    </h3>
    <blockquote>
     <ol>
      <li>
       整理和式数列为
       <img alt="\sum_{i=1}^{kn}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bkn%7D"/>
       的形式
      </li>
      <li>
       在
       <img alt="\sum_{i=1}^{kn}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bkn%7D"/>
       内部对数列通项整理(一般是同除或同乘n,
       <img alt="n^{2}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?n%5E%7B2%7D"/>
       ,...
       <img alt="n^{N}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?n%5E%7BN%7D"/>
       ,
       <img alt="N\in N^{+}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?N%5Cin%20N%5E%7B&amp;plus;%7D"/>
       )。
      </li>
      <li>
       将所有含
       <img alt="\frac{i}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7Bi%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
       的项看做x,
       <img alt="\frac{i}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7Bi%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
       外嵌套数列通项公式看做函数法则,那么便得到了(3)式。
      </li>
      <li>
       实际转换时我们只需要按照
       <img alt="\frac{i}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7Bi%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
       为核心去化简整理即可。
      </li>
     </ol>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     例.
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\sum_{i=1}^{2n}\frac{ln(n^2+i^2)-2ln(n)}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B2n%7D%5Cfrac%7Bln%28n%5E2&amp;plus;i%5E2%29-2ln%28n%29%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     解：原式=
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\sum_{i=1}^{2n}\frac{ln(n^2+i^2)-ln(n^2)}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B2n%7D%5Cfrac%7Bln%28n%5E2&amp;plus;i%5E2%29-ln%28n%5E2%29%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     原式=
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\sum_{i=1}^{2n}\frac{ln(1+(\frac{i}{n})^2)}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B2n%7D%5Cfrac%7Bln%281&amp;plus;%28%5Cfrac%7Bi%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5E2%29%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      求和是针对i的,
      <img alt="\frac{1}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
      在求和符号内相当于常数
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p>
     原式=
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\frac{1}{n}\sum_{i=1}^{2n}{ln(1+(\frac{i}{n})^2)}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7B2n%7D%7Bln%281&amp;plus;%28%5Cfrac%7Bi%7D%7Bn%7D%29%5E2%29%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     到这儿,我们便将这个数列极限整理成了非常完美的形式,并且还能一眼看出积分下限为0
    </p>
    <p>
     被积函数为
     <img alt="ln(1+x^2)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?ln%281&amp;plus;x%5E2%29"/>
     ,接下来就是判断上限了……
    </p>
    <h2>
     公式法判定上下限
    </h2>
    <p>
     根据符合定积分定义的和式数列的通项判定积分上下限是一项必备技能,倘若你遇到类似题目,发现可以使用定积分定义来做,但是却迟迟找不出积分上下限,那么这就太可惜了。
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}\sum_{i=1}^{kn}f(a+\frac{(b-a)i}{kn})\frac{b-a}{kn}=\int_{a}^{b}f(x)dx" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7D%5Csum_%7Bi%3D1%7D%5E%7Bkn%7Df%28a&amp;plus;%5Cfrac%7B%28b-a%29i%7D%7Bkn%7D%29%5Cfrac%7Bb-a%7D%7Bkn%7D%3D%5Cint_%7Ba%7D%5E%7Bb%7Df%28x%29dx"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     以下是我自己总结的一个方法：
    </p>
    <h3>
     判定方法
    </h3>
    <blockquote>
     <ol>
      <li>
       定位到数列通项中含
       <img alt="\frac{i}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7Bi%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
       的一坨式子,如(3)式中的
       <img alt="a+\frac{(b-a)i}{kn}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?a&amp;plus;%5Cfrac%7B%28b-a%29i%7D%7Bkn%7D"/>
       ，这里我们设它为
       <img alt="g(n,i)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?g%28n%2Ci%29"/>
       ，积分上下限分别为a,b,若g(n,i)中具有常数项,那么常数项为积分下限a，否则就是0。
      </li>
      <li>
       令g(n,i)中的i分别为求和符号的上下限,一般而言是1和kn，那么a=
       <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}g(n,1)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7Dg%28n%2C1%29"/>
       ，b=
       <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}g(n,kn)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7Dg%28n%2Ckn%29"/>
       ,由此便确定了积分的上下限。
      </li>
      <li>
       求和符号外含
       <img alt="\frac{1}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
       的式子我们称他为步长,那么既然已经有了积分上下限,那么这个步长也就呼之欲出了,步长显然就是
       <img alt="\frac{b-a}{kn}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7Bb-a%7D%7Bkn%7D"/>
       。
      </li>
     </ol>
    </blockquote>

    <p>

     <img alt="\frac{i}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7Bi%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
     ,求和符号上下限分别为
     <img alt="" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?"/>
     1与2n

    <p>
     令i=1,
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}g(n,1)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7Dg%28n%2C1%29"/>
     =0;
    </p>
    <p>
     令i=2n,
     <img alt="\lim_{n\to\infty}g(n,2n)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Clim_%7Bn%5Cto%5Cinfty%7Dg%28n%2C2n%29"/>

     那么显而易见,该极限对应的定积分上下限分别为0,2步长为

     <img alt="\frac{1}{n}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cfrac%7B1%7D%7Bn%7D"/>
