    <p>
     探序基因 整理
    </p>
    <p>
     进入百度网盘官网
     <a href="https://pan.baidu.com/download#pan" rel="nofollow" title="百度网盘 客户端下载">
      百度网盘 客户端下载
     </a>
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:justify">
     下载linux的rpm格式的安装包
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     <img alt="" height="140" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/37fcc3e7279b4ab1b00ad60cf2859829.png" width="457"/>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     在linux命令行中输入：rpm -ivh baidunetdisk_4.17.7_x86_64.rpm
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     出现报错：
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     错误：依赖检测失败：
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     libXScrnSaver 被 baidunetdisk-4.17.7-1.x86_64 需要
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     于是：
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     yum install libXScrnSaver, yum install libXScrnSaver-devel
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     再rpm -ivh baidunetdisk_4.17.7_x86_64.rpm就可以了
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     安装bypy命令行工具
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     pip3.9 install bypy --user
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     (--user的意思，是安装在用户目录下，通常是
     <code>
      ~/.local/bin
     </code>
     （对于可执行脚本）)
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     重置关联账号：rm -rf ~/.bypy
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     #获取授权码并关联账号
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     ~/.local/bin/bypy info
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     #该命令会生成一个 URL，你需要在浏览器中打开并获取授权码，然后粘贴回命令行。
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     #关联成功后，可以运行以下命令查看账号信息：
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     ~/.local/bin/bypy info
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     下载：~/.local/bin/bypy downfile  网盘文件   文件夹
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:36pt; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     提交后台下载nohup  ~/.local/bin/bypy downfile  网盘文件   文件夹 &amp;
    </p>
