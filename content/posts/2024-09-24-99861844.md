    <p>
     最近写了一个2048游戏，写完之后觉得没有音效不够完整，就开始琢磨着怎么添加音效，这里给出添加方法。
     <br/>
     1.首先将音乐添加到资源文件中
     <br/>
     2.创建QMediaPlayer对象，要包含头文件
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-cpp">QMediaPlayer <span class="token operator">*</span>startSound <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token keyword">new</span> QMediaPlayer<span class="token punctuation">;</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     然后我们可以通过该类的方法添加音乐，设置音量
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-cpp">startSound<span class="token operator">-</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token function">setMedia</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token function">QUrl</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token string">"qrc:/img/bell.mp3"</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
    startSound<span class="token operator">-</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token function">setVolume</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token number">50</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     这里要说一下，之前我是这样写的，结果是没有音效
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-cpp">startSound<span class="token operator">-</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token function">setMedia</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token function">QUrl</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token string">":/img/bell.mp3"</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//无法播放</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     在网上搜了各种解决方案，都没有用，真是崩溃了，最后找到另一个人的代码，发现可以播放音乐，这才知道完全就是代码的问题，哪需要什么播放器，不过现在我还不明白为什么一定要把qrc加上才可以，因为我在添加图片的时候就一直没有带这个qrc的，而且还可以正常显示，这个问题就不深究了，记住加qrc就好了，我们继续。
     <br/>
     设置背景音乐我们需要一首音乐循环播放，这里我们就要用到头文件，这里要明白它和QMediaPlayer的联系，这QMediaPlayList是一个音乐列表，然后你可以设置音乐播放是循环还是随机还是一次结束，而QMediaPlayer是控制音乐的播放，暂停，音量，理解了他们的关系，代码就很容易了
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-cpp">  QMediaPlayer <span class="token operator">*</span>gameSound <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token keyword">new</span> QMediaPlayer<span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//创建播放器</span>
  QMediaPlaylist <span class="token operator">*</span>gameList <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token keyword">new</span> QMediaPlaylist<span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//创建播放列表</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     这里我的背景音乐是单曲循环，当然播放列表也可以添加多首音乐
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-cpp">  gameList<span class="token operator">-</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token function">addMedia</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token function">QUrl</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token string">"qrc:/img/game.mp3"</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//添加音乐</span>
  gameList<span class="token operator">-</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token function">setPlaybackMode</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>QMediaPlaylist<span class="token operator">::</span>CurrentItemInLoop<span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//循环播放</span>
</code></pre>
    <pre><code class="prism language-cpp">gameSound<span class="token operator">-</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token function">play</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//音乐播放</span>
gameSound<span class="token operator">-</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token function">stop</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span><span class="token comment">//音乐暂停</span>
</code></pre>
