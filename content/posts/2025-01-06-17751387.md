       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
        常用开源桌面GIS软件
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
        <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
         QGIS
        </span>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; color:rgb(0,0,0)">
        <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
         <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
          始于2002年5月，算得上是开源GIS平台中的后起之秀。界面友好，分析功能可与GRASS GIS相媲美。
          <br style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial"/>
         </span>
        </span>
        主页：
        <a href="http://www.qgis.org/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; text-decoration:none; color:rgb(255,119,0); border-bottom-width:0px; border-bottom-style:dotted; border-bottom-color:rgb(51,51,51); border-top-width:0px; border-right-width:0px; border-left-width:0px; border-style:initial; border-color:initial" target="_blank">
         http://www.qgis.org/
        </a>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
        <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
         GRASS GIS
        </span>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       源于1982年美国军方（陆军工程兵团的一个分支——美国陆军建筑工程研究实验室）的实际应用，重量级的开源桌面GIS软件。分析功能强悍，但界面简陋，很多操作是以命令的方式运行的，喜欢命令式操作的朋友可以尽情享受。
       <br style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial"/>
       主页：
       <a href="http://grass.fbk.eu/index.php" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; text-decoration:none; color:rgb(255,119,0); border-bottom-width:0px; border-bottom-style:dotted; border-bottom-color:rgb(51,51,51); border-top-width:0px; border-right-width:0px; border-left-width:0px; border-style:initial; border-color:initial" target="_blank">
        http://grass.fbk.eu/index.php
       </a>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
        <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
         MapWinGIS
        </span>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; font-size:14px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">
        <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
         MapWinGIS是一个不错的开源GIS项目，开发语言是VC，基于微软的COM思想编写。其核心库是一个名字为MapWinGIS的ActiveX控件，
        </span>
       </span>
       功能上类似MapX，开发人员可以利用这个ActiveX组件在自己的系统中添加GIS的相关功能。其开发团队
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; font-size:14px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">
        在MapWinGIS 的基础上，二次开发了MapWindow GIS，这是一套GIS系统，用户可以直接通过它浏览数据，并通过一系列插件来完成其他功能。还有一套MapWinX，是DOTNET平台下的一个辅助工具。
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; font-size:14px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">

         http://www.mapwindow.org/
        </a>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
        <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
         DotSpatial
        </span>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; font-size:14px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">
        DotSpatial是基于.Net 4的一个开源GIS库，它提供有一个可视化的
        <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
         map
        </span>
        控件，可在Windows Forms或者Web Application上展示地图。能够方便的支持shapefile、grid及其它栅格/影像数据的读写，在图形渲染方面，提供了较多实用的接口，开发人员可充分定制地图的渲染效果。支持数据的投影处理以及一些常用的分析功能。
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; font-size:14px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">

        <a href="http://dotspatial.codeplex.com/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; text-decoration:none; color:rgb(255,119,0); border-bottom-width:0px; border-bottom-style:dotted; border-bottom-color:rgb(51,51,51); border-top-width:0px; border-right-width:0px; border-left-width:0px; border-style:initial; border-color:initial" target="_blank">
         http://dotspatial.codeplex.com/
        </a>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
        <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; font-size:14px; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
         MapWindow 6
        </span>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       MapWindow 6是基于DotSpatial而构建的一套插件式桌面GIS系统。对于开发人员，可基于DotSpatial定制个性化的插件来扩展其功能。
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; color:rgb(0,0,255)">
        <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
         <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; font-size:14px">
          World Wind
         </span>
        </span>
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
       <span style="margin-top:0px; margin-right:0px; margin-bottom:0px; margin-left:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-right:0px; padding-bottom:0px; padding-left:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial; font-size:14px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">
        World Wind是由NASA推出的三维GIS软件，背景可是相当厉害。适合大数据量的影响发布与三维可视化。
       </span>
      </p>
      <p style="color:rgb(51,51,51); font-size:15px; line-height:1.8; margin-top:10px; margin-right:auto; margin-bottom:10px; margin-left:auto; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; outline-width:0px; outline-style:none; outline-color:initial">
