    <p>
     <span style="color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);">
      <span style="font-size:16px;">
       <strong>
        round 函数很简单，对浮点数进行近似取值，保留几位小数。比如
       </strong>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="language-python">round(10.0/3, 2)
#结果：3.33
round(20/7)
#结果：3</code></pre>
    <p>
     <span style="color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);">
      <span style="font-size:12px;">
       第一个参数是一个浮点数，第二个参数是保留的小数位数，可选，如果不写的话默认保留到整数。
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;">
      <span style="font-size:16px;">
       <span style="margin:0px;padding:0px;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;">
        <span style="margin:0px;padding:0px;line-height:18px;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);">
         <strong>
          1、round的结果跟python版本有关
         </strong>
        </span>
       </span>
       <br/>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);">
      <span style="margin:0px;padding:0px;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);">
       <span style="margin:0px;padding:0px;line-height:18px;background-color:rgb(255,255,0);">
        <span style="color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);">
         <span style="font-size:12px;">
          python2和python3中有什么不同：
         </span>
        </span>
        <br/>
       </span>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="language-python">#python2
#Python 2.7.8 (default, Jun 18 2015, 18:54:19) 
#Type "help", "copyright", "credits" or "license" for more information.
round(0.5)
#结果为：1.0</code></pre>
    <pre><code class="language-python">#python3
#Python 3.4.3 (default, Oct 14 2015, 20:28:29) 
#Type "help", "copyright", "credits" or "license" for more information.
round(0.5)
#结果为：0</code></pre>
    <p style="margin:10px auto;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);font-size:12px;">
     我们阅读一下python的文档，里面是这么写的：
    </p>
    <p style="margin:10px auto;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);font-size:12px;">
     在python2.7的doc中，round()的最后写着，“Values are rounded to the closest multiple of 10 to the power minus
     <span style="margin:0px;padding:0px;">
      ndigits
     </span>
     ; if two multiples are equally close, rounding is done away from 0.” 保留值将保留到离上一位更近的一端（四舍六入），如果距离两端一样远，则保留到离0远的一边。所以round(0.5)会近似到1，而round(-0.5)会近似到-1。
    </p>
    <p style="margin:10px auto;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);font-size:12px;">
     但是到了python3.5的doc中，文档变成了“values are rounded to the closest multiple of 10 to the power minus
     <span style="margin:0px;padding:0px;">
      ndigits
     </span>
     ; if two multiples are equally close, rounding is done toward the even choice.” 如果距离两边一样远，会保留到偶数的一边。比如round(0.5)和round(-0.5)都会保留到0，而round(1.5)会保留到2。
    </p>
    <p style="margin:10px auto;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);font-size:12px;">
     所以如果有项目是从py2迁移到py3的，可要注意一下round的地方（当然，还要注意/和//，还有print，还有一些比较另类的库）。
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="margin:0px;padding:0px;color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;">
      <span style="margin:0px;padding:0px;line-height:18px;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);">
       <span style="font-size:16px;">
        <strong>
         2、特殊数字round出来的结果可能未必是想要的。
        </strong>
       </span>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="language-python">round(2.675, 2)
#结果为：2.67</code></pre>
    <p>
     <span style="color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);font-size:12px;">
      python2和python3的doc中都举了个相同的栗子，原文是这么说的：
     </span>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="language-python">Note

The behavior of round() for floats can be surprising: for example, round(2.675, 2) 
gives 2.67 instead of the expected 2.68. This is not a bug: it’s a result of the fact 
that most decimal fractions can’t be represented exactly as a float. See Floating 
Point Arithmetic: Issues and Limitations for more information.</code></pre>
    <span style="color:rgb(0,0,0);font-family:verdana, 'ms song', '宋体', Arial, '微软雅黑', Helvetica, sans-serif;text-align:left;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);font-size:12px;">
     简单的说就是，round(2.675, 2) 的结果，不论我们从python2还是3来看，结果都应该是2.68的，结果它偏偏是2.67，为什么？这跟浮点数的精度有关。我们知道在机器中浮点数不一定能精确表达，因为换算成一串1和0后可能是无限位数的，机器已经做出了截断处理。那么在机器中保存的2.675这个数字就比实际数字要小那么一点点。这一点点就导致了它离2.67要更近一点点，所以保留两位小数时就近似到了2.67。
    </span>
    <br/>
