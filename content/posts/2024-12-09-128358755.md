    <p>
     在微信小程序开发过程中，有些页面底部按钮是固定在底部的，还有如果是自定义tabbar也是需要固定在底部的，这里有两个问题：
    </p>
    <ul>
     <li>
      不同机型的底部安全区域不同，如有些苹果手机底部有操作栏，这部分距离需要留出来否则会遮挡内容
     </li>
     <li>
      如果底部有固定定位元素，可能会导致内容区域底部部分元素被固定元素遮挡
     </li>
    </ul>
    <h2>
     <a id="1__4">
     </a>
     问题1 解决方案
    </h2>
    <p>
     利用IOS新增的
     <code>
      env()
     </code>
     和
     <code>
      constant()
     </code>
     特性即可解决，开发者不需要自己动态计算高度，只需将如下CSS代码添加至样式中：
    </p>
    <p>
     示例代码：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-css"><span class="token selector">.bottom-wrap</span> <span class="token punctuation">{<!-- --></span>
	<span class="token property">position</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span> fixed<span class="token punctuation">;</span>
	<span class="token property">bottom</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span> 0<span class="token punctuation">;</span>
	<span class="token property">padding-bottom</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span> <span class="token function">constant</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>safe-area-inset-bottom<span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
	<span class="token property">padding-bottom</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span> <span class="token function">env</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>safe-area-inset-bottom<span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
<span class="token punctuation">}</span>
</code></pre>
    <h2>
     <a id="2__17">
     </a>
     问题2 解决方案
    </h2>
    <p>
     内容区域设置
     <code>
      padding-bottom
     </code>
     ，如果底部固定定位元素的高度是
     <code>
      50px
     </code>
     ，那么只需要设置css：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-css"><span class="token selector">.content-wrap</span> <span class="token punctuation">{<!-- --></span>
   <span class="token property">padding-bottom</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span> <span class="token function">calc</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token function">constant</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>safe-area-inset-bottom<span class="token punctuation">)</span> + 50px<span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
   <span class="token property">padding-bottom</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span> <span class="token function">calc</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token function">env</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>safe-area-inset-bottom<span class="token punctuation">)</span> + 50px<span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
<span class="token punctuation">}</span>
</code></pre>
