     1. 登录:oc login
    </span>
    <br style="font-family:dashicons, 'Segoe UI', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei Mono', 'Microsoft Yahei', 'Microsoft Yahei Mono', '微软雅黑', sans-serif;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);"/>

     2. 创建新项目:oc new-project ProjectName   (ProjectName为新建的项目名)
    </span>
    <br style="font-family:dashicons, 'Segoe UI', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei Mono', 'Microsoft Yahei', 'Microsoft Yahei Mono', '微软雅黑', sans-serif;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);"/>

     3. 查看当前项目下的pod: oc get pod
    </span>
    <br style="font-family:dashicons, 'Segoe UI', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei Mono', 'Microsoft Yahei', 'Microsoft Yahei Mono', '微软雅黑', sans-serif;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);"/>

     4. 查看project: oc get project
    </span>
    <br style="font-family:dashicons, 'Segoe UI', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei Mono', 'Microsoft Yahei', 'Microsoft Yahei Mono', '微软雅黑', sans-serif;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);"/>

     <span style="font-family:dashicons, 'Segoe UI', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei Mono', 'Microsoft Yahei', 'Microsoft Yahei Mono', '微软雅黑', sans-serif;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);">

     6. 将一个服务映射出域名: oc expose svc svcname –hostname=xxx,将一个服务器映射成域名，支持http/https协议，svcname可以不写默认与dcname相同
    </span>

     7. 查看pod日志: oc logs -f podname
    </span>
    <br style="font-family:dashicons, 'Segoe UI', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei', 'WenQuanYi Micro Hei Mono', 'Microsoft Yahei', 'Microsoft Yahei Mono', '微软雅黑', sans-serif;background-color:rgb(255,255,255);"/>
