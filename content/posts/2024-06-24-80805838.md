    <p>
     springBoot项目遇到了“
     <span style="color:#ff0000;">
      Handler dispatch failed; nested exception is java.lang.StackOverflowError
     </span>
     ”的错误。
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#ff0000;">
      总结一哈：
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     StackOverflowError通常情况下是死循环或者是循环依赖了。
    </p>
    <pre class="has"><code class="language-java">Caused by: java.lang.StackOverflowError
	at com.hry.seckill.service.impl.GoodsServiceImpl.getGoodsVoList(GoodsServiceImpl.java:17)
	at com.hry.seckill.service.impl.GoodsServiceImpl.getGoodsVoList(GoodsServiceImpl.java:17)
	at com.hry.seckill.service.impl.GoodsServiceImpl.getGoodsVoList(GoodsServiceImpl.java:17)
	at com.hry.seckill.service.impl.GoodsServiceImpl.getGoodsVoList(GoodsServiceImpl.java:17)
	at com.hry.seckill.service.impl.GoodsServiceImpl.getGoodsVoList(GoodsServiceImpl.java:17)
	at com.hry.seckill.service.impl.GoodsServiceImpl.getGoodsVoList(GoodsServiceImpl.java:17)</code></pre>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     然后检查定位到的位置，发现错误：
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="language-html hljs">@Service
public class GoodsServiceImpl implements GoodsService {
    @Autowired
    private GoodsService goodsService;</code></pre>
    <p>
     <span style="color:#ff0000;">
      我在Service中没有正确引用编写的Mapper而是引用了Service，所以报错，改正即可。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="language-html hljs">@Service
public class GoodsServiceImpl implements GoodsService {
    @Autowired
    private GoodsMapper goodsMapper;</code></pre>
    <p>
     最后重启项目即可。
    </p>
