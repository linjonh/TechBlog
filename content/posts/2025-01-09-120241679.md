    <p>
     <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/df0aecbd46208a0a92d7e12fbec46b8d.png"/>

    <h2>
     <a id="4__35">
     </a>
     4 修改代码适配硬件
    </h2>
    <p>
     打开示例代码，可以看到其实是支持ssd1306和sh1106的，两者需要进行区分。
    </p>
    <p>
     1 修改头文件和参数类型
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-c"><span class="token comment">//sh1106</span>
<span class="token macro property"><span class="token directive-hash">#</span><span class="token directive keyword">include</span> <span class="token string">"SH1106Wire.h"</span></span>
SH1106Wire <span class="token function">display</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token number">0x3c</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> SDA<span class="token punctuation">,</span> SCL<span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>   

<span class="token comment">//SSD1306</span>
<span class="token macro property"><span class="token directive-hash">#</span><span class="token directive keyword">include</span> <span class="token string">"SSD1306Wire.h"</span></span>
SSD1306Wire <span class="token function">display</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token number">0x3c</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> SDA<span class="token punctuation">,</span> SCL<span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>   
</code></pre>
    <p>
     需要注意，有点驱动设置0x3c为SH1106，0x3D为SSD1306，看驱动定义。
    </p>
    <p>
     2 修改SDA SCL
     <br/>
     如第二章介绍的，查看电路图知道了对应的IO口
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-c"><span class="token comment">//sh1106</span>
<span class="token macro property"><span class="token directive-hash">#</span><span class="token directive keyword">include</span> <span class="token string">"SH1106Wire.h"</span></span>
SH1106Wire <span class="token function">display</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token number">0x3c</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token number">23</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token number">22</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>   

<span class="token comment">//SSD1306</span>
<span class="token macro property"><span class="token directive-hash">#</span><span class="token directive keyword">include</span> <span class="token string">"SSD1306Wire.h"</span></span>
SSD1306Wire <span class="token function">display</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token number">0x3c</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token number">23</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token number">22</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>   
</code></pre>

     至此修改就完成了，尝试下运行示例，是否成功。

     后续可以根据提供的示例修改显示内容。
