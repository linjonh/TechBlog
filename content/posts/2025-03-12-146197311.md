    <h2>
     <a id="Workerman_1">
     </a>
     一、安装Workerman依赖
    </h2>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      使用Composer安装Workerman至TP6项目
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">composer</span> require workerman/workerman   //workman主框架
<span class="token function">composer</span> require workerman/channel		//基于订阅的多进程通讯组件，用于workerman进程间通讯或者服务器集群通讯，类似redis订阅发布机制。基于workerman开发
</code></pre>
    <h2>
     <a id="_10">
     </a>
     二、创建自定义命令
    </h2>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      生成WebSocket服务启动命令
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash">php think make:command Workerman   

</code></pre>
    <p>
     需在config/console.php中注册指令‌
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token string">'workerman'</span> <span class="token operator">=</span><span class="token operator">&gt;</span> <span class="token punctuation">\</span>app<span class="token punctuation">\</span>command<span class="token punctuation">\</span>Workerman::class,
</code></pre>
    <h2>
     <a id="_24">
     </a>
     三、运行命令
    </h2>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
