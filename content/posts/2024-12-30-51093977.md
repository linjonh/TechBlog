       <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">

       </span>

     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">

      </span>

     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important; font-size:14px; color:rgb(61,167,66)">

      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important; font-size:14px">
       粒度若建得太细，连网线、内存条都变成配置项，最后CMDB中存储的70%数据没有作用，只是做了大量无用功。
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important; font-size:14px; color:rgb(61,167,66)">
       . 缺少行业实践参考
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important; font-size:14px">
       国内很多时候都是根据BMC、HP等模型来建立一个模型库，但实际上老外的思路与国人迥异，往往会做出过于复杂的模型体系。
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important; font-size:14px; color:rgb(61,167,66)">

      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important; font-size:14px">
       使用关系型数据库，模型中每一个类型的属性都是一个列，最后调整总是要动用研发，完成一次调整需要2天的时间，而这种调整在数据补充阶段，往往要经常进行，耗时耗力。
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">

      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">
       <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important; font-size:14px; color:rgb(0,0,0)">
        我们怎么干的– 管理
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important; font-size:14px; color:rgb(61,167,66)">
       . 目标驱动
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important; font-size:14px">
       持续迭代的方式推进，只实现当前目标需要的最小模型集合。建议不要使用传统软件研发大瀑布模式来建设模型，而是使用持续迭代的方式，每次都设定一下较小的目标，按这个目标去建立刚好满足要求的模型库。
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; max-width:100%!important; word-wrap:break-word!important">

      </span>
     </p>
