    <h2>
     一、简介
    </h2>
    <p>
     使用知识图谱嵌入模型，将三元组（h,r,t）中关系 r 转化为平移、旋转、缩放矩阵对头节点以及尾节点进行运算，判定三元组的真实性。
    </p>
    <h2>
     二、原理
    </h2>
    <h3>
     1.整体
    </h3>
    <p>
     CompoundE评分函数：
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="f(h,t)=||T_r\cdot R_r\cdot S_r\cdot h- \widehat{T_r} \cdot \widehat{R_r} \cdot \widehat{S_r} \cdot t||" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?f%28h%2Ct%29%3D%7C%7CT_r%5Ccdot%20R_r%5Ccdot%20S_r%5Ccdot%20h-%20%5Cwidehat%7BT_r%7D%20%5Ccdot%20%5Cwidehat%7BR_r%7D%20%5Ccdot%20%5Cwidehat%7BS_r%7D%20%5Ccdot%20t%7C%7C"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     其中 h , t 表示头部和尾部实体嵌入，
     <img alt="T_r" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?T_r">
      、
      <img alt="R_r" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?R_r">
       、
       <img alt="S_R" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?S_R">
        表示头部实体嵌入的平移、旋转、缩放操作。
        <img alt="\widehat{T_r}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cwidehat%7BT_r%7D">
         、
         <img alt="\widehat{R_r}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cwidehat%7BR_r%7D">
          、
          <img alt="\widehat{S_r}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Cwidehat%7BS_r%7D">
           表示尾部实体嵌入的平移、旋转、缩放操作。由于矩阵乘法是不可交换的，不同计算顺序会产生不同性能的CompoundE，这个看附录B。
          </img>
         </img>
        </img>
       </img>
      </img>
     </img>
    </p>
    <h3>
     2.单个矩阵
    </h3>
    <p>
     二维平移矩阵可以写成
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="T=\begin{bmatrix} 1&amp; 0 &amp;v_x \\ 0 &amp; 1 &amp;v_y \\ 0 &amp; 0 &amp; 1 \end{bmatrix}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?T%3D%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D%201%26%200%20%26v_x%20%5C%5C%200%20%26%201%20%26v_y%20%5C%5C%200%20%26%200%20%26%201%20%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     二维旋转矩阵可以写成
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="R=\begin{bmatrix} \cos(\theta) &amp; -\sin( \theta) &amp;0 \\ \sin( \theta) &amp; \cos(\theta) &amp; 0\\ 0&amp; 0 &amp; 1 \end{bmatrix}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?R%3D%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D%20%5Ccos%28%5Ctheta%29%20%26%20-%5Csin%28%20%5Ctheta%29%20%260%20%5C%5C%20%5Csin%28%20%5Ctheta%29%20%26%20%5Ccos%28%5Ctheta%29%20%26%200%5C%5C%200%26%200%20%26%201%20%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     二维缩放矩阵可以写成
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="S=\begin{bmatrix} s_x &amp; 0&amp;0 \\ 0&amp; s_y &amp;0 \\ 0&amp; 0 &amp; 1 \end{bmatrix}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?S%3D%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D%20s_x%20%26%200%260%20%5C%5C%200%26%20s_y%20%260%20%5C%5C%200%26%200%20%26%201%20%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     因此，复合矩阵可以表示为
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="T \cdot R \cdot S=\begin{bmatrix} s_x\cos(\theta)&amp; -s_y\sin(\theta) &amp;v_x \\ s_x\sin(\theta)&amp; s_y\cos(\theta) &amp;v_y \\ 0&amp; 0 &amp; 1 \end{bmatrix}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?T%20%5Ccdot%20R%20%5Ccdot%20S%3D%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D%20s_x%5Ccos%28%5Ctheta%29%26%20-s_y%5Csin%28%5Ctheta%29%20%26v_x%20%5C%5C%20s_x%5Csin%28%5Ctheta%29%26%20s_y%5Ccos%28%5Ctheta%29%20%26v_y%20%5C%5C%200%26%200%20%26%201%20%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     当
     <img alt="s_x=0" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?s_x%3D0"/>
     和
     <img alt="s_y=0" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?s_y%3D0"/>
     时，复合矩阵是可逆的。它可以写成
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="M^{-1}=\begin{bmatrix} A^{-1}&amp; -A^{-1}v\\ 0&amp; 1 \end{bmatrix}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?M%5E%7B-1%7D%3D%5Cbegin%7Bbmatrix%7D%20A%5E%7B-1%7D%26%20-A%5E%7B-1%7Dv%5C%5C%200%26%201%20%5Cend%7Bbmatrix%7D"/>
    </p>
    <h3>
     3.与其他距离KGE模型的关系
    </h3>
    <p>
     文章中讲述可以使用CompoundE中特定形式的矩阵，从CompoundE的评分函数中派生出TransE、RotatE、LinearRE、PairRE的评分函数。
    </p>
    <h3>
     4.Compound的损失函数
    </h3>
    <p>
     损失函数可以写为
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="L_{KGE}=-\log \sigma (\zeta _1-f_r(h,t))-\sum_{i-1}^{n}\rho(h_{i}^{'},r,t_{i}^{'}) \log \sigma (f_r(h_{i}^{'},t_{i}^{'})-\zeta _1)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?L_%7BKGE%7D%3D-%5Clog%20%5Csigma%20%28%5Czeta%20_1-f_r%28h%2Ct%29%29-%5Csum_%7Bi-1%7D%5E%7Bn%7D%5Crho%28h_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%2Cr%2Ct_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%29%20%5Clog%20%5Csigma%20%28f_r%28h_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%2Ct_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%29-%5Czeta%20_1%29"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     其中
     <img alt="\sigma" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Csigma"/>
     是sigmoid函数，
     <img alt="\zeta_1" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Czeta_1"/>
     是一个固定边界超参数，
     <img alt="(h_{i}^{'},r,t_{i}^{'})" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%28h_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%2Cr%2Ct_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%29"/>
     是第 i 个负三元组，
     <img alt="p(h_{i}^{'},r,t_{i}^{'})" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?p%28h_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%2Cr%2Ct_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%29"/>
     是负三元组得到的概率。给正三元组
     <img alt="(h_i,r,t_i)" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%28h_i%2Cr%2Ct_i%29"/>
     ，负采样分布为
    </p>
    <p style="text-align:center">
     <img alt="p(h_{j}^{'},r,t_{j}^{'} | \{ (h_i,r,t_i)\} ) = \frac{ \exp \alpha_1 f_r(h_{j}^{'},t_{j}^{'})}{\sum_i \exp \alpha_1 f_r(h_{i}^{'},t_{i}^{'})}" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?p%28h_%7Bj%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%2Cr%2Ct_%7Bj%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%20%7C%20%5C%7B%20%28h_i%2Cr%2Ct_i%29%5C%7D%20%29%20%3D%20%5Cfrac%7B%20%5Cexp%20%5Calpha_1%20f_r%28h_%7Bj%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%2Ct_%7Bj%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%29%7D%7B%5Csum_i%20%5Cexp%20%5Calpha_1%20f_r%28h_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%2Ct_%7Bi%7D%5E%7B%27%7D%29%7D"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="\alpha_1" class="mathcode" src="https://latex.csdn.net/eq?%5Calpha_1"/>
     是采样温度。
    </p>
