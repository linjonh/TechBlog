    <h2 style="margin-left:0px; margin-right:0px; text-align:justify">
     服务器更新
    </h2>
    <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     RD服务器更新了，更新的文件放在190的D:\Temp\CHTeam\fnehr_update_20170106\下了。
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     数据库更新:
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     1、在Hosp库中执行hosp.sql。
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     另: Hosp库中执行下面的sql语句，调整service_dt。注意set @acct_code 。
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     update BillingJob set service_dt= srve_dt from PatVisit where PatVisit.case_no=BillingJob.case_no and PatVisit.mr_no=BillingJob.mr_no and  srve_dt is not null and PatVisit.srve_dt&lt;&gt;BillingJob.service_dt
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     declare @acct_code varchar(4)
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     set  @acct_code ='0009'
    </p>
    <p style="margin-left:0; margin-right:0; text-align:justify">
     update GENEDBS..BillingJobPool set service_dt=BillingJob.service_dt from BillingJob, GENEDBS..BillingJobPool as BillingJobPool where acct_code=@acct_code and BillingJob.job_no=BillingJobPool.job_no
    </p>
