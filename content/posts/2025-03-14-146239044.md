   <div class="htmledit_views" id="content_views">
    <p>
     问题：
    </p>
    <p>
     在vsCode中安装python的ollama包，执行命令
    </p>
    <pre><code>pip install ollama</code></pre>
    <p>
     遇到错误：
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      ERROR: pip's dependency resolver does not currently take into account all the packages that are installed. This behaviour is the source of the following dependency conflicts.
      <br/>
      ollama-python 0.1.2 requires requests&lt;3.0.0,&gt;=2.31.0, which is not installed.
      <br/>
      ollama-python 0.1.2 requires httpx&lt;0.27.0,&gt;=0.26.0, but you have httpx 0.28.1 which is incompatible.
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     就是这样：
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="499" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/8ecfc9cdecd844058fd7e760f4f9e80d.png" width="867">
      然后尝试命令pip install httpx==0.26.0后，又不符合ollama对httpx的版本要求，所以这个httpx不是和ollama-python版本冲突就是和ollama版本冲突，最终卸载重来，包括捣乱的ollama-python：
     </img>
    </p>
    <pre><code>pip uninstall ollama
pip uninstall ollama-python</code></pre>
    <p>
     然后再安装ollama，就通过了
    </p>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="499" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/c48efa08a218442aabed682ccd174205.png" width="1251"/>
    </p>
