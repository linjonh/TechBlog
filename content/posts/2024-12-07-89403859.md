   <div class="htmledit_views" id="content_views">
    <p>
     1.选择information_schema
    </p>
    <pre class="has"><code>use information_schema</code></pre>
    <p>
     2.查看information_schema表信息
    </p>
    <pre class="has"><code>desc tables;</code></pre>
    <p>
     3.查看数据库所有库的schema
    </p>
    <pre class="has"><code>select distinct table_schema from tables;</code></pre>
    <p>
     4.查看指定数据库的物理内存
    </p>
    <pre class="has"><code>select concat(round(sum(DATA_LENGTH/1024/1024),2),'MB') as data from TABLES where table_schema='db_name';</code></pre>
    <p>
     注意：db_name一般是数据库的名称，对应步骤3查询的table_schema
    </p>
    <p>
     5.查看指定数据库指定表的物理内存
    </p>
    <pre class="has"><code>select concat(round(sum(DATA_LENGTH/1024/1024),2),'MB') as data from TABLES where table_schema='db_name' and table_name='table_name';</code></pre>
