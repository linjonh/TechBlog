    <h2>
     TP中使用cast + order
    </h2>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="136" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/05a2bf7c4edf4beb84513c47e1a205a8.png" width="712"/>
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      $lists = AmdCommonTable::where(..............)
     </p>
     <p>
      -&gt;field('*,CAST(w6 AS UNSIGNED) as sort')
     </p>
     <p>
      -&gt;order('sort', 'asc')
     </p>
     <p>
      -&gt;select()
     </p>
     <p>
      -&gt;toArray();
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     先转换为数字，再order by
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <h2>
     效果对比
    </h2>
    <p>
     <strong>
      <span style="color:null">
       (1/2)
      </span>
      <span style="color:#fe2c24">
       不ok
      </span>
     </strong>
     - 直接order by 某字段 asc -
     <span style="color:#fe2c24">
      只能按照文本排序
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     其中w6字段类型：`w6` TEXT
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      SELECT id,w6 FROM `xx` ORDER BY `w6` ASC
     </p>
    </blockquote>
    <p>
     <img alt="" height="270" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/9f1addea3be04ec0a87394447b2f5e3d.png" width="231"/>
    </p>
    <p>
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      <span style="color:null">
       (2/2)
      </span>
      <span style="color:#fe2c24">
      </span>
     </strong>
     <span style="color:#fe2c24">
      <strong>
       ok
      </strong>
     </span>
     - 使用cast方法转换“文本”字段为"数字"，再order by排序
    </p>
    <blockquote>
     <p>
      SELECT *,CAST(w6 AS UNSIGNED) as sort FROM `xx` ORDER BY `sort` ASC
     </p>
    </blockquote>
