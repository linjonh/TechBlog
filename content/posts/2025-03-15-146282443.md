    <h3>
     <a id="Docker_compose_27">
     </a>
     二、Docker compose配置
    </h3>
    <p>
     卷映射
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-yaml">
<span class="token punctuation">-</span> ./neo4j/config<span class="token punctuation">:</span>/var/lib/neo4j/conf  
<span class="token punctuation">-</span> ./neo4j/plugins<span class="token punctuation">:</span>/var/lib/neo4j/plugins

</code></pre>
    <p>
     配置文件：
     <br/>
     有两个配置文件
    </p>
    <p>
     neo4j.conf
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-conf">server.memory.pagecache.size=512M  
server.default_listen_address=0.0.0.0  
  
dbms.security.procedures.unrestricted=apoc.*  
server.directories.logs=/logs
</code></pre>
    <p>
     apoc.conf
     <br/>
     用于备份和恢复
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-conf">apoc.import.file.enabled=true  
apoc.export.file.enabled=true  
apoc.ttl.enabled=false  
apoc.uuid.enabled=false
</code></pre>
    <p>
    </p>
    <h3>
     <a id="_68">
     </a>
     三、重启容器
    </h3>
    <pre><code class="prism language-shell"><span class="token function">docker</span> compose up

访问：http://localhost:7474/browser/

</code></pre>
    <p>
     测试函数：
