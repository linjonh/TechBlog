    <h4 style="padding:0px; margin:0px; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:21px; font-family:宋体">
      1.
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
       前言
      </span>
     </span>
    </h4>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; text-indent:32px; text-align:justify; line-height:21px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      随着手机技术不断发展，iPhone(iOS)以及Android等智能手机普及速度加快，越来越多的人选择使用手机终端
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      进行
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      各种
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      音视频，比如视频聊天、视频会议、远程医疗、
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      IM、在线开户等
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      。根据市场研究机构comScore发布的最新消息，2011年8月，在美国Android和iOS设备控制了71%的智能手机市场份额
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      ，
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      所以开发
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      APP
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      是需要的，也是必要的。
     </span>
    </p>
    <h4 style="padding:0px; margin:0px; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:21px; font-family:宋体">
      2.
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
       开发要求
      </span>
     </span>
    </h4>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; text-indent:36px; text-align:justify; line-height:21px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      无论是开发iOS APP还是Android APP，都需要各自的SDK开发工具包。其中iOS限制较多，Android则相对开放。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; text-indent:36px; text-align:justify; line-height:21px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      <strong>
       2.1 iOS开发要求
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; text-indent:36px; text-align:justify; line-height:21px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      根据iOS开发中心的资料，iOS开发工具Xcode需要在苹果公司自己的Mac OS设备之上运行，无法在Windows环境下运行改程序。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; text-indent:36px; text-align:justify; line-height:21px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      与此同时，iOS APP的发行需要购买99美元的授权。否则只可以在模拟器上模拟运行程序，无法在iPhone、iPad设备上实际运行测试。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      <strong>
       2.2 iOS传播途径
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      iOS的应用程序使用iOS终端设备登陆Apple App Store进行下载。软件开发方可设置是否收费。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      <strong>
       2.3 Android开发要求
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      相对于iOS，Android SDK开发工具更为开放，且可以在Windows平台正常安装运行。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      但相比iOS终端设备的统一性（iPhone3S,iPhone4,iPhone4S,iPad,iPad2）而言，Android手机分辨率各不相同，分辨率兼容性问题严重。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      2.4 Android传播途径
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      在中国大陆地区Android传播途径有两种：
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      第一种是通过类似于Apple App Store的Android Market（安卓市场）进行下载，下载形式及付费形式与iOS类似。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:36px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      第二种则是通过一些网站直接进行下载安装，如“豌豆荚”、“机锋市场”等网站。
     </span>
    </p>
    <h4 style="padding:0px; margin:0px; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:21px; font-family:宋体">
      3
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:21px; font-family:宋体">
      .
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
       技术实现
      </span>
     </span>
    </h4>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      <strong>
       需求
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:37px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      iOS开发需要购置Mac OS系统的电脑（Apple）作为开发软件的基础环境，同时需要iPhone3、iPhone4、iPad、iPad2等不同分辨率的设备来进行实际使用测试。同时需要花费99美元购买Apple Developer商业授权。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:37px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      Android开发只需普通PC即可，无需授权费用。但需要不同分辨率的Android手机进行实际测试。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      <strong>
      </strong>
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      <strong>
       人员
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      手机APP开发无法一个人完成，需要至少3人的团队进行专项开发。（测试人员1，开发人员2，测试人员兼开发。）
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      <strong>
       技术
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      因为iOS终端设备（如iPhone）不支持Flash等播放器，所以iOS联播技术需要将视频流转换为M3U8变种格式。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      Android则有更好的兼容性，可直接播放流媒体服务器输出的视频。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:32px; text-align:justify">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      因此需要流媒体服务器单独为iOS终端设立一个视频流，以保证iOS和Android终端的同时需求。
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      而为了更好的开发，还需要一款音视频解决方案，笔者推荐大家使用
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      AnyChat，
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      它
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      是一套跨平台的即时通讯解决方案，基于先进的H.264视频编码标准、AAC音频编码标准与P2P技术，支持高清视频，整合了佰锐科技在音
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      视频编码、多媒体通讯领域领先的开发技术和丰富的产品经验而设计的高质量、宽适应性、分布式、模块化的网络音视频互动平台。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:32px; text-align:justify">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      ·Any`Cha`t 所涉及的技术领域包括：
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:32px; text-align:justify">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      1、视频处理技术：视频采集、编码、传输、解码及显示；
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:32px; text-align:justify">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      2、音频处理技术：音频采集、编码、传输、解码及显示；
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:32px; text-align:justify">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      3、音频预处理技术：静音检测（VAD）、噪音抑制（NS）、自动增益控制（AGC）以及回音消除（AEC）等；
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:32px; text-align:justify">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      4、流媒体传输技术：音频抖动缓冲（Jitter Buffer）、视频马赛克消除（MS）；
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:32px; text-align:justify">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      5、P2P网络技术：UDP NAT、TCP NAT、P2P多径路由算法（MPA）；
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; overflow:hidden; color:rgb(85,85,85); font-family:宋体,'Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:32px; text-align:justify">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px; font-family:宋体">
      6、服务器并发处理技术；

    </p>
