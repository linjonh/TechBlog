    <h2>
     <a id="4_17">
     </a>
     4、安装前准备
    </h2>
    <h3>
     <a id="41root_gbasedbt__18">
     </a>
     4.1、以root用户创建 gbasedbt 组和用户
    </h3>
    <p>
     创建用户组
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">groupadd</span> gbasedbt
</code></pre>
    <p>
     创建用户
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">useradd</span> -g gbasedbt gbasedbt -s /bin/bash -m
</code></pre>
    <p>
     为 gbasedbt 用户设置密码
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">passwd</span> gbasedbt
</code></pre>
    <h3>
     <a id="42_GBase_8s__33">
     </a>
     4.2、创建 GBase 8s 数据库安装目录
    </h3>
    <p>
     GBase 8s 数据库缺省的安装目录为 /opt/GBASE/gbase，并且此文件夹必须为空。
     <br/>
     本文设定安装目录是/home/gbasedbt/GBASE/gbase
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">mkdir</span> -p /home/gbasedbt/GBASE/gbase
</code></pre>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">chown</span> -R gbasedbt:gbasedbt /home/gbasedbt/GBASE/gbase
</code></pre>
    <h3>
     <a id="43_44">
     </a>
     4.3、上传并解压安装包
    </h3>
    <p>
     解压缩到/home/gbasedbt/目录下
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">unzip</span> x86+RHEL6.zip -d /home/gbasedbt/
</code></pre>
    <p>
     进入安装包目录
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token builtin class-name">cd</span> /home/gbasedbt/x86+RHEL6/GBase8sV8.8_3.3.0_2_RHEL6_x86_64/
</code></pre>
    <p>
     解开tar包
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">mkdir</span> GBase8sV8.8_TL_3.3.0_2_36477d_RHEL6_x86_64
</code></pre>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">tar</span> -xvf GBase8sV8.8_TL_3.3.0_2_36477d_RHEL6_x86_64.tar -C GBase8sV8.8_TL_3.3.0_2_36477d_RHEL6_x86_64/
</code></pre>

     5.1、执行安装程序

     以 root 身份登录，进入解压目录，执行要安装的产品的安装脚本 ids_install，进行安装
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token builtin class-name">cd</span> /home/gbasedbt/x86+RHEL6/GBase8sV8.8_3.3.0_2_RHEL6_x86_64/GBase8sV8.8_TL_3.3.0_2_36477d_RHEL6_x86_64
</code></pre>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">sh</span> ids_install
</code></pre>
