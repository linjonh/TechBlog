    <p>
     <strong>
      解决办法如下：
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p>
     修改
     <code>
      jre/lib/security/java.security
     </code>
     中的
     <code>
      disabledAlgorithms
     </code>
     ，删除
     <code>
      SSLv3, TLSv1, TLSv1.1
     </code>
     ，然后重启应用即可。在vim下，可以使用
     <code>
      /disabledAlgorithms
     </code>
     快速查找。
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-java">jdk<span class="token punctuation">.</span>tls<span class="token punctuation">.</span>disabledAlgorithms<span class="token operator">=</span>RC4<span class="token punctuation">,</span> DES<span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token class-name">MD5withRSA</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> \
    DH keySize <span class="token operator">&lt;</span> <span class="token number">1024</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> EC keySize <span class="token operator">&lt;</span> <span class="token number">224</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token number">3D</span>ES_EDE_CBC<span class="token punctuation">,</span> anon<span class="token punctuation">,</span> NULL<span class="token punctuation">,</span> \
    include jdk<span class="token punctuation">.</span>disabled<span class="token punctuation">.</span>namedCurves
</code></pre>
    <p>
     如果修改了，保存了，重启了还是没有效果，
     <strong>
      就看下面这个方法
     </strong>
     ：
    </p>
    <p>
     修改jdk配置文件
     <code>
      /etc/crypto-policies/back-ends/java.config
     </code>
     ，类似上面方法一样，删除`SSLv3, TLSv1, TLSv1.1``，保存重启应用。
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-java">jdk<span class="token punctuation">.</span>tls<span class="token punctuation">.</span>disabledAlgorithms<span class="token operator">=</span>DH keySize <span class="token operator">&lt;</span> <span class="token number">2048</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token class-name">SSLv2</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token class-name">SSLv3</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token class-name">TLSv1</span><span class="token number">.1</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> DHE_DSS<span class="token punctuation">,</span> RSA_EXPORT<span class="token punctuation">,</span> DHE_DSS_EXPORT<span class="token punctuation">,</span> DHE_RSA_EXPORT<span class="token punctuation">,</span> DH_DSS_EXPORT<span class="token punctuation">,</span> DH_RSA_EXPORT<span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token class-name">DH_anon</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token class-name">ECDH_anon</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span> DH_RSA<span class="token punctuation">,</span> DH_DSS<span class="token punctuation">,</span> ECDH<span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token number">3D</span>ES_EDE_CBC<span class="token punctuation">,</span> DES_CBC<span class="token punctuation">,</span> RC4_40<span class="token punctuation">,</span> RC4_128<span class="token punctuation">,</span> DES40_CBC<span class="token punctuation">,</span> RC2<span class="token punctuation">,</span> <span class="token class-name">HmacMD5</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     另外
     <strong>
      jdk奇数版本可用，偶数版本有上面这个问题
     </strong>
     ，可用通过
     <code>
      yum --showduplicate list java* | grep 1.8.0
     </code>
     查看可用的版本，选择奇数版本安装也能解决这个问题。
    </p>
