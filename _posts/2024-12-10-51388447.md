    <h1 class="title-article" id="articleContentId">
     “云”到底是什么
    </h1>
   </div>
  </div>
 </div>
 <article class="baidu_pl">
  <div class="article_content clearfix" id="article_content">

    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     最近几年云的概念被炒的热火朝天，类似阿里云，腾讯云之类，之后各家科技公司都推出云平台，感觉无论什么东西，加上云的概念，就瞬间高大上一样。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     ​
     <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <img alt="" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/83fb196c0124de7fd81cc823f15c1669.jpeg" style="border:0px; max-width:100%; height:auto!important; margin:0px; padding:0px">
      <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
     </img>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     导致现在大家看到云平台，不仅心中已经不起波澜，可能还会深深的鄙视一下,云一下子被玩烂了的感觉。特别是一些公司，业务和云完全不搭边，也会跟随潮流，强行云一把。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     今天我们就来聊聊目前主流的云有哪些形式，让你火眼金睛，一眼看穿一些公司宣传的伪云。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <img alt="" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/f445612a0dabcde2b0d62026dfb8e952.jpeg" style="border:0px; max-width:100%; height:auto!important; margin:0px; padding:0px">
      <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
     </img>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px">
      IAAS(Infrastructure-as-a-service)
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     中文全称：基础设施即服务。IAAS形式的主要利润点是硬件设施。比如目前国内的阿里云，号称基于阿里的8000台服务器，双11时供阿里服务，闲时供普通客户使用。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px">
      PAAS(Platform-as-a-service)
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     中文全称：平台即服务。和IAAS简单粗暴提供硬件设施不同，PAAS是在硬件的基础上提供存储服务，Server服务。例如亚马逊的AWS服务，提供可动态扩展的存储服务，国内的新浪SAE平台，提供AppEngine服务。推荐新浪SAE平台，屌丝程序员可以免费搭建一个自己的系统玩玩！记得，是免费的哦。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     IAAS和PAAS，能提供这两种服务的企业，绝对是庞然大物，最少是上市的科技公司吧！大家想一下，不在中国有自己的数据中心，好意思同别人讲，我能提供类似阿里云的服务？
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; line-height:1.6">
      所以IAAS和PAAS的准入门槛非常高，这就直接过滤90%以上的企业了。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     既然多数的企业都被IAAS和PAAS的高门槛过滤掉了，那现在随处可见的云产品，又是什么呢？ 基本市面上见到的云都是SAAS了。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px">
      SAAS（Software-as-a-service）
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     中文全称：软件即服务。故名思议，是提供软件服务的。简单了讲：就是注册用户，缴了费用，就可以使用软件了，SAAS软件相对于传统的软件，优势不言而喻，实施成功本低，按需收费等等。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     例如目前流行的CRM提供商salesforce，国内云OA eteam，金蝶提供的云ERP服务等等。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <img alt="" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/bd6fd5c0c1ab061cedf88a9bcad0772c.jpeg" style="border:0px; max-width:100%; height:auto!important; margin:0px; padding:0px">
      <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; line-height:1.6; widows:auto">
       SAAS的准入门槛很低，只要你有软件，放在互联网上，用户注册使用，就可以称之为云服务了。
      </span>
     </img>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     企业都是以利润为导向，从利润的角度来看各种云的不同。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     IAAS主要通过提供硬件来获得利润；
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; widows:auto">
      PAAS基于IAAS，提供更高一层的存储、Server服务等来获取利润；
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     而SAAS则是主要通过提供软件服务来获取利润；
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     现在我们来看看，如果一个企业宣称自己有云平台或者云计算，但是并没有提供这三种服务中的任何一个，就有强行云的嫌疑了。
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     <br style="margin:0px; padding:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
     当然，还有另外一种可能，他们的服务是部署在阿里云平台上的，所以就成了云平台!
    </p>
    <p style="font-family:'Helvetica Neue',Helvetica,'Hiragino Sans GB','Microsoft YaHei',Arial,sans-serif; font-size:16px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; clear:both; line-height:25.6px">
