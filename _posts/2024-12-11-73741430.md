    <p style="text-indent:28px">
     <span style="font-family:'宋体'; font-size:14px">
      <span style="font-family:'宋体'">
       检查系统处理器是否支持虚拟机的硬件加速
      </span>
     </span>

    <p style="text-indent:28px">
     <span style="font-family:'宋体'; font-size:14px">
      egrep -c '(vmx|svm)' /proc/cpuinfo

       libvirt
      </span>

       取代
      </span>
      <span style="font-family:Calibri">
       KVM
      </span>
      <span style="font-family:'宋体'">

      </span>
      <span style="font-family:Calibri">
       QEMU
      </span>
     </span>

      </span>
     </span>

     <span style="font-family:'宋体'">
      <span style="font-size:14px">

     </span>
