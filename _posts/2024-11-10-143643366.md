    <h3>
     <a id="_16">
     </a>
     替换数组变量
    </h3>
    <p>
     要使用
     <code>
      envsubst
     </code>
     替换 YAML 中的数组变量
     <code>
      CHAT_MODELS
     </code>
     ，可以按照以下步骤进行操作：
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      设置环境变量
     </strong>
     ：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token builtin class-name">export</span> <span class="token assign-left variable">CHAT_MODELS</span><span class="token operator">=</span><span class="token string">"test-72b-instruct,test-yi-34b-chat-0812"</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     <strong>
      创建一个模板文件
      <code>
       template.yaml
      </code>
     </strong>
     ，内容如下：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-yaml"><span class="token key atrule">chat_models</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span> <span class="token punctuation">[</span>$<span class="token punctuation">{<!-- --></span>CHAT_MODELS<span class="token punctuation">}</span><span class="token punctuation">]</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     <strong>
      使用
      <code>
       envsubst
      </code>
      进行替换
     </strong>
     ：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash">envsubst <span class="token operator">&lt;</span> template.yaml <span class="token operator">&gt;</span> output.yaml
</code></pre>
    <p>
     生成的
     <code>
      output.yaml
     </code>
     文件内容将是：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-yaml"><span class="token key atrule">chat_models</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span> <span class="token punctuation">[</span>test<span class="token punctuation">-</span>72b<span class="token punctuation">-</span>instruct<span class="token punctuation">,</span> test<span class="token punctuation">-</span>yi<span class="token punctuation">-</span>34b<span class="token punctuation">-</span>chat<span class="token punctuation">-</span><span class="token number">0812</span><span class="token punctuation">]</span>
</code></pre>
    <h3>
     <a id="_YAML__44">
     </a>
     如果你希望以 YAML 数组的格式输出
    </h3>
    <p>
     <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/direct/13d7a2e77d8f475eb80c2be43c98243b.png#pic_center"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     为了将逗号分隔的字符串转换为每个元素单独一行的格式，你可以使用
     <code>
      tr
     </code>
     命令：
    </p>
    <p>
     <strong>
      将逗号替换为空格并准备模板
     </strong>
     ：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token builtin class-name">export</span> <span class="token assign-left variable">CHAT_MODELS</span><span class="token operator">=</span><span class="token string">"test-72b-instruct,test-yi-34b-chat-0812"</span>
<span class="token builtin class-name">echo</span> <span class="token string">"chat_models:"</span> <span class="token operator">&gt;</span> template.yaml
<span class="token builtin class-name">echo</span> <span class="token string">"  - <span class="token variable">${CHAT_MODELS<span class="token operator">/</span><span class="token operator">/</span><span class="token operator">,</span><span class="token operator">/</span>\\n  - }</span>"</span> <span class="token operator">&gt;&gt;</span> template.yaml
</code></pre>
    <p>
     <strong>
      然后可以将模板内容输出到文件
     </strong>
     ：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-bash"><span class="token function">cat</span> template.yaml <span class="token operator">&gt;</span> output.yaml
</code></pre>
    <p>
     生成的
     <code>
      output.yaml
     </code>
     文件将是：
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-yaml"><span class="token key atrule">chat_models</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span>
  <span class="token punctuation">-</span> test<span class="token punctuation">-</span>72b<span class="token punctuation">-</span>instruct
  <span class="token punctuation">-</span> test<span class="token punctuation">-</span>yi<span class="token punctuation">-</span>34b<span class="token punctuation">-</span>chat<span class="token punctuation">-</span><span class="token number">0812</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     这样，你就可以成功将环境变量
     <code>
      CHAT_MODELS
     </code>
     替换为符合 YAML 格式的数组。
    </p>
