     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; border:0px">
      2017年12月7-9日，中国
      <a href="http://www.99lt.com/bigdata/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; color:rgb(11,59,140)" target="_blank">
       <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1">
        大数据
       </span>
      </a>
      技术大会（Big Data Technology Conference 2017，BDTC 2017）将在北京新云南皇冠假日酒店隆重举行。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style='padding-top:10px; padding-bottom:20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; text-indent:0em; color:rgb(25,25,25); line-height:1.7; font-size:16px; border:0px; font-family:"PingFang SC",Arial,微软雅黑,宋体,simsun,sans-serif; text-align:justify'>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; border:0px">
      2008年，作为中国大数据技术大会的前身，第一届Hadoop中国云计算会议在中科院计算所举行。连续成功举办十年的中国大数据盛宴，让我们见证了大数据技术生态在中国的建立、发展和成熟。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style='padding-top:10px; padding-bottom:20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; text-indent:0em; color:rgb(25,25,25); line-height:1.7; font-size:16px; border:0px; font-family:"PingFang SC",Arial,微软雅黑,宋体,simsun,sans-serif; text-align:justify'>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; border:0px">
      大会就大数据时代社会各行业的智能化进程和行业实践展开深入讨论。除Keynote外，主办方精心策划了15场专题技术和行业论坛，涵盖了
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; font-weight:700; border:0px">
       大
       <a href="http://www.99lt.com/bigdata/bigdata-analysis/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; color:rgb(11,59,140)" target="_blank">
        <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1">
         数据分析
        </span>
       </a>
       与生态系统、数据库、大数据云服务、
       <a href="http://www.99lt.com/ai/machine-learning/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; color:rgb(11,59,140)" target="_blank">
        <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1">
         机器学习
        </span>
       </a>
       与
       <a href="http://www.99lt.com/ai/deep-learning/" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; color:rgb(11,59,140)" target="_blank">
        <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1">
         深度学习
        </span>
       </a>
       、
       <a href="http://www.99lt.com/plus/search.php?kwtype=0&amp;q=%D6%AA%CA%B6%" rel="nofollow noopener noreferrer" style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; color:rgb(11,59,140)" target="_blank">
        <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1">
         知识图谱
        </span>
       </a>
       、区块链、推荐系统、金融大数据、交通与旅游大数据、工业与制造业大数据、精准医疗大数据、大数据安全与政策法规、数据科学与大数据技术教育
      </span>
      等主题。此外，为期近一年的
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; font-weight:700; border:0px">
       TOP10大数据应用最佳案例的最终评选结果
      </span>
      ，也将在大会上揭晓。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style='padding-top:10px; padding-bottom:20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; text-indent:0em; color:rgb(25,25,25); line-height:1.7; font-size:16px; border:0px; font-family:"PingFang SC",Arial,微软雅黑,宋体,simsun,sans-serif; text-align:justify'>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; border:0px">
      近30位行业专家组成的论坛主席团和大会程序委员会，就会议的主题策划、讲师邀约、议题审核等方面层层把关，力求为与会者带来一场高水平的大数据技术盛会。届时，超过120位国内外技术专家将为现场千名以上的大数据行业精英、技术专家及意见领袖带来100多场技术演讲。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style='padding-top:10px; padding-bottom:20px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; text-indent:0em; color:rgb(25,25,25); line-height:1.7; font-size:16px; border:0px; font-family:"PingFang SC",Arial,微软雅黑,宋体,simsun,sans-serif; text-align:justify'>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; border:0px">
      为让参会者对大会内容有更全面的了解，
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; outline:0px; zoom:1; font-weight:700; border:0px">
       今天，BDTC 2017大会完整议程正式公布。
