     错误提示：
    </p>
    <pre><code>### Cause: java.sql.SQLException: Error setting driver on UnpooledDataSource. Cause: java.lang.ClassNotFoundException: Cannot find class: ${driver}
</code></pre>

      <br/>
      <code>
       &lt;configuration&gt;

      </code>
