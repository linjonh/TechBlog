    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     执行#Python与#python -V，看到版本号是2.4.3，很老了，而且之前写的都是跑在python3.X上面的，3.X和2.X有很多不同，
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     有兴趣的朋友可以参考下这篇文章：
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     http://www.jb51.net/article/34011.htm
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     更新python千万不要把老版本的删除！新老版本是可以共存的，很多基本的命令、
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     软件包都要依赖预装的老版本python的，比如yum。
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     一、升级到2.7.3
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     1. 升级安装
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     首先下载源tar包
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     可利用linux自带下载工具wget下载，如下所示：
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # wget http://www.python.org/ftp/python/2.7.3/Python-2.7.3.tar.gz
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     下载完成后到下载目录下，解压
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # tar -zxvf Python-2.7.3.tar.
    </span>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     gz
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     进入解压缩后的文件夹
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     cd Python-2.7.3
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     在编译前先在/usr/local建一个文件夹python2.7.3（作为python的安装路径，以免覆盖老的版本）
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # mkdir /usr/local/python2.7.3
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     在解压缩后的目录下编译安装
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # ./configure --prefix=/usr/local/python2.7.3
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # make
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #
    </span>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     make install
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     此时没有覆盖老版本，再将原来/usr/bin/python链接改为别的名字
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # mv /usr/bin/python /usr/bin/python_old
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     再建立新版本python的链接
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # ln -s /usr/local/python2.7.3/bin/python2.7 /usr/bin/python
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     这个时候输入
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # python
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">

    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # make install
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     到此，程序就能正常运行了；
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     二、升级到3.3.0
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     更新python：
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     第1步：更新gcc，
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     因为gcc版本太老会导致新版本python包编译不成功 代码如下:
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #yum -y install gcc
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     系统会自动下载并安装或更新，等它自己结束
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     第2步：下载Python-3.3.0软件包
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     代码如下:
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     # wget --no-check-certificate http://python.org/ftp/python/3.3.0/Python-3.3.0.tar.bz2
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     注意：按照上述命令下载的软件包会存放在你当前的工作目录下，
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     wget命令是一个从网络上自动下载文件的自由工具，具体用法，
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     请参考这篇文章：http://www.jb51.net/os/RedHat/73089.html
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     说明：命令中的数字就是版本号，你也可以把3.3.0换成你需要的版本.
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     第3步：解压已下载的二进制包并编译安装
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     代码如下:
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #tar -jxvf Python-3.3.0.tar.bz2
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #cd Python-3.3.0
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #./configure
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #make all
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #make install
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #make clean
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #make distclean
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>

     # /usr/local/bin/python3 –V

    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     编译安装完毕以后，可以输入上面一行命令，查看版本
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">

    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">

     #ln -s /usr/local/bin/python3.3 /usr/bin/python

     输入

    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     #python -V，
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     即可查看当前默认python版本
    </span>
    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>
    <span style="font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px">
     默认的python成功指向3.3.0以后，yum不能正常使用，需要修改yum的配置文件

    <br style="word-wrap:break-word; font-family:'sans serif',tahoma,verdana,helvetica; font-size:16px; line-height:24px"/>

     第5步：修改yum配置文件
