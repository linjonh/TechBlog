       </p>
      </li>
      <li>
       <p>
        -d ignore home config files for autoscan and fast mode
       </p>
      </li>
      <li>
       <p>
        -S enable sleep time supression betwen each request (hardcore mode)

      </li>

      <li>
       <p>
        -L in parsable output mode (-P), continue listening after the active scan is completed
       </p>
