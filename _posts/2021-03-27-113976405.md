     <p align="center">
      <img alt="a78e09d42bf10b5b17401faaad00a580.png" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/99d22cb2ffe2f2e00e6cd97ffc10b18f.jpeg"/>
     </p>
     <p>
      Python字典换行的方法如下：
     </p>
     <p>
      1、换行时保证行尾是逗号即可
     </p>
     <p>
      a = {"key0": "val0",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key1": "val1",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key2": "val2"}
     </p>
     <p>
      2、在长度不影响阅读的情况下这种写法也是允许的：
     </p>
     <p>
      a = {"key0": "val0", "key1": "val1",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key2": "val2", "key3": "val3",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key4": "val4", "key5": "val5"}
     </p>
     <p>
      注意这种情况下，每一行第一个非空白字符都要和第一行的括号后的字符对齐，并且最后的括号是不换行(直接跟在最后一个元素后面)的。
     </p>
     <p>
      3、另外一种换行方法如下：
     </p>
     <p>
      "key0": "val0",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key1": "val1",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key2": "val2"
     </p>
     <p>
      }
     </p>
     <p>
      a = {
      <!-- -->
     </p>
     <p>
      "key0": "val0",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key1": "val1",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key2": "val2"
     </p>
     <p>
      }
     </p>
     <p>
      或是
     </p>
     <p>
      a = {
      <!-- -->
     </p>
     <p>
      "key0": "val0", "key1": "val1",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key2": "val2", "key3": "val3",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key4": "val4", "key5": "val5"
     </p>
     <p>
      }
     </p>
     <p>
      a = {
      <!-- -->
     </p>
     <p>
      "key0": "val0", "key1": "val1",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key2": "val2", "key3": "val3",
     </p>
     <p>
      "key4": "val4", "key5": "val5"
     </p>
     <p>
      }
     </p>
     <p>
      这种情况下，内容的每一行都需要比第一行只多一层缩进(即4个空格)
     </p>
