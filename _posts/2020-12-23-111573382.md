    <h2>
     <a id="PostgreSQL_0">
     </a>
     PostgreSQL查看版本信息
    </h2>
    <p>
     <strong>
      1.查看客户端版本
     </strong>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-clike">psql <span class="token operator">--</span>version
</code></pre>
    <p>
     <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/df1f6988c65977b319da4ca872c3d4e3.png">
      <br/>
      <strong>
       2.查看服务器端版本
      </strong>
      <br/>
      2.1 查看详细信息
     </img>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-clike">select <span class="token function">version</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/a9a439e31df00dfcf5a37645b6ac56ac.png">
      <br/>
      2.2 查看版本信息
     </img>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-clike">show server_version<span class="token punctuation">;</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     <img alt="在这里插入图片描述" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/9ec9fa6b017ebf4266de4b4604e7543c.png">
      <br/>
      2.2 查看数字版本信息包括小版号
     </img>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-clike">SHOW server_version_num<span class="token punctuation">;</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     或
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-clike">SELECT <span class="token function">current_setting</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token string">'server_version_num'</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     <strong>
      3.注意事项
     </strong>
     <br/>
     SELECT current_setting(‘server_version_num’);返回类型为text，如果需要可以转换为interger
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-clike">SELECT <span class="token function">current_setting</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token string">'server_version_num'</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span><span class="token punctuation">:</span>integer<span class="token punctuation">;</span>
</code></pre>
