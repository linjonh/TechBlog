    <p>
     在ubuntu上刚开始默认的是Python2,要想从Python2切换到Python3,进行如下配置即可:
    </p>
    <h3>
     <a id="1_Python2Python3_2">
     </a>
     1 Python2切换到Python3
    </h3>
    <pre><code>echo alias python=python3 &gt;&gt; ~/.bashrc
source ~/.bashrc
</code></pre>
    <h3>
     <a id="2_Python3Python2_6">
     </a>
     2 Python3切换到Python2
    </h3>
    <pre><code>gedit  ~/.bashrc
alias python=python3 
source ~/.bashrc
</code></pre>
    <h3>
     <a id="3__11">
     </a>
     3 查看版本
    </h3>
    <pre><code>python -V
