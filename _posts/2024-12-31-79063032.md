         4、分析

     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">

     </p>

      （1）我们的大团队中包含网络运维（简称运维）、平台研发（简称开发）、一线运维（简称一线）以及
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">

     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      （2）经过早期团队标准化和自动化工作的推进，运维已经具备了全员写脚本的能力，如果可以，他们更愿意自己写脚本，而非到开发那里提需求、排期，因为这样
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        太慢
       </span>
      </strong>
      了。
     </p>

     </p>

      （3）经过早期人工运维的时期，运维已经掌握了大量的运维经验，沉淀出很多的大大小小的运维场景，每种场景需要的自动化能力是不一样的，所以如果能自己实现，运维仍然是不愿意去交给开发实现的，因为这样不仅慢，而且还可能要
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">

       </span>
      </strong>
      。

      <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
     </p>

       </span>
      </strong>

     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      其实
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        Forward用户只需要关注
       </span>
      </strong>
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        虚线以下的部分
       </span>
      </strong>
      ，拿到Forward返回的标准类实例后，考虑给哪个设备执行什么功能，先后顺序怎么安排，结果怎么处理等等，其他都不需要再关心了。
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      到目前为止，只剩一个元素没有说明白的了，那就是标准类库/标准类实例，它们是用户得以快速编排场景的前提，接下来就对它们做一个介绍。
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
     </p>
     <h3 style="font-weight: bold;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <span style="max-width: 100%;font-size: 17px;color: rgb(255, 192, 0);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       7、统一类库
      </span>
     </h3>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <span style="max-width: 100%;font-size: 17px;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;font-size: 16px;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        之前提到
        <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
         <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
          类库层是核心
         </span>
        </strong>
        ，我们是
        <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
         <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(255, 104, 39);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
          为了屏蔽底层网络设备异构差异、统一格式
         </span>
        </strong>
        而设置的类库层，那么类库具体是什么样子？
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <span style="max-width: 100%;font-size: 17px;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;font-size: 16px;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
       </span>
      </span>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;text-align: center;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <img class="img_loading" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/af51ce75416ceb5680518c531ae6a1bb.jpeg" style="box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; width: 670px !important; visibility: visible !important; height: 360.17895490336434px !important;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      这张图表示了Forward类库层的简化继承关系，
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        以用户管理功能为视角，可以看到从上至下的属性/方法分布，可以避免重复开发
       </span>
      </strong>
      。到了最末枝的类节点时，已经有很明显的功能区别了，根据设备种类的不同，可能会包含交换器/路由器/防火墙等不同专有方法。
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;text-indent: 0em;white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        经过统一类库封装，不同设备添加用户的指令就简化为统一的“addUser()”方法
       </span>
      </strong>
      了，这是一个比较大的进步，因为我们批量添加300个账户的任务可以简化成一个几行代码的脚本了：
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <img class="img_loading" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/0f67f57a5456516273bab28d5eb5b49a.jpeg" style="box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; width: 670px !important; visibility: visible !important; height: 302.18567251461985px !important;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      用户甚至都不需要知道这三种设备添加账户的指令是什么，因为它们已经封装在不同的类库中了！
     </p>
     <h3 style="font-weight: bold;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);white-space: normal;line-height: 1.6em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
     </h3>
     <h3 style="font-weight: bold;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;font-size: 17px;color: rgb(255, 192, 0);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        8、总结
       </span>
      </strong>
     </h3>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      Forward的实现思路分享结束了，实现它并不难，但是需要持续投入力量去扩充类库方法以及文档。
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <img class="img_loading" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/62eadcb5537de9108fb9f826dffbe15f.jpeg" style="box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; width: 670px !important; visibility: visible !important; height: 399.21991701244815px !important;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      我习惯称呼这张图为“
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        生态图
       </span>
      </strong>
      ”，它表示了使用Forward的网络运维生态圈，开发与运维和谐相处，每个人都有发挥空间，现在
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        Forward各类封装场景实例调用次数已经达到万级，小到IP查询，大到故障定位都有它的身影
       </span>
      </strong>
      。
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      结局：有了Forward助力网络运维后，
      <span style="max-width: 100%;text-decoration: line-through;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       老板更加无视了我们的加人请求
      </span>
      ，老板更加相信了网络运维团队的能力，然后接维了更多的网络设备…
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;text-align: center;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <img class="img_loading" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/529ced05a71b1faa98931878521af957.png" style="box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; width: 161px !important; visibility: visible !important; height: 159px !important;"/>
     </p>
     <h3 style="font-weight: bold;max-width: 100%;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);white-space: normal;text-align: center;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      之后还要做什么
     </h3>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      说来说去，Forward也仅仅是一个模块而已，参考一个运维自研水平的套路：
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;text-align: center;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        手工 -&gt; 脚本 -&gt; 工具 -&gt; 平台 -&gt; 智能
       </span>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <strong style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       <span style="max-width: 100%;color: rgb(253, 102, 37);box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
        <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
       </span>
      </strong>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      Forward顶多可以算到工具这个级别，而在团队中和Forward同级别的运维模块已经有4个了，一个比一个棒，在上层不做一个平台就太对不起它们了，试想如果能用浏览器和鼠标完成各种运维操作，
      <span style="max-width: 100%;text-decoration: line-through;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       那就能前移更多的工作给一线了
      </span>
      ，那就能更高效率的完成工作了：
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <br style="max-width: 100%;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;"/>
     </p>
     <p style="max-width: 100%;min-height: 1em;color: rgb(62, 62, 62);font-size: 16px;white-space: normal;line-height: 1.75em;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
      <img class="img_loading" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/e8c9307c9a01ea71b5a1e4c6aef338a5.jpeg" style="box-sizing: border-box !important; word-wrap: break-word !important; width: 670px !important; visibility: visible !important; height: 364.6291012838802px !important;"/>
      <span style="max-width: 100%;text-align: center;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       比如说前面提到的批量添加300个账户的任务，
      </span>
      <span style="max-width: 100%;text-indent: 28px;box-sizing: border-box !important;word-wrap: break-word !important;">
       这个平台也已经实现了，我们会放到后面的文章中介绍，欢迎继续关注“火炉山蚁群”！
      </span>
     </p>
