    </h1>

   <div class="htmledit_views" id="content_views">
    <p>
     pip是Python官方建议的包安装工具。可以使用pip快速安装python支持的大部分模块，下面讲解怎么安装pip
    </p>
    <p>
     工具/原料
    </p>
    <p>
     python、pip
    </p>
    <p>
     win7
    </p>
    <p>
     方法/步骤
    </p>
    <p>
     1
    </p>
    <p>
     在安装pip之前，首先确认你的python环境正常，然后通过官网下载pip安装包。
    </p>
    <p align="center">
     <img alt="6a408cdd3340b6f3008e96a912c0affce086eea2.jpg?x-bce-process=image%2Fresize%2Cm_lfit%2Cw_500%2Climit_1" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/416238b87d9a0a262d2a4f6cb6caa5df.png"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     2
    </p>
    <p>
     将下载的包解压出来，然后通过win+R快捷方式打开命令提示符窗口，并在命令提示符中进入到pip文件目录下。
    </p>
    <p align="center">
     <img alt="939c2d40b6f3918791150c7524fce186252fefa2.jpg?x-bce-process=image%2Fresize%2Cm_lfit%2Cw_500%2Climit_1" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/5f00f563c5db9a84588eabe1f303b983.png"/>
    </p>
    <p align="center">
     <img alt="3201a8f39187031c0ac93a496a86242fa972eca2.jpg?x-bce-process=image%2Fresize%2Cm_lfit%2Cw_500%2Climit_1" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/68fda0e579d29939ea8a1eec844d8185.png"/>
    </p>
    <p align="center">
     <img alt="b7b28f87031c99c03bf57433af2fa872951feda2.jpg?x-bce-process=image%2Fresize%2Cm_lfit%2Cw_500%2Climit_1" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/515871054c21a10d4aac329684a641bf.png"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     3
    </p>
    <p>
     在pip目录下，输入命令"python setup.py install"进行pip模块安装；安装完成后会有"Finished"字样。
    </p>
    <p align="center">
     <img alt="90c61d1c99c0affc748fb19a2372941fbfe4eaa2.jpg?x-bce-process=image%2Fresize%2Cm_lfit%2Cw_500%2Climit_1" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/429de6694b0b3b9063d187b72436e46d.png"/>
    </p>
    <p align="center">
     <img alt="a31e1214c27bd2824f8034f33cb1eef97ebd36a3.jpg?x-bce-process=image%2Fresize%2Cm_lfit%2Cw_500%2Climit_1" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/3a2b7e3363858256e0e1496926843b3c.png"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     4
    </p>
    <p>
     待安装完成后，在命令提示符中输入"pip list"或者"pip -help"，如果有相应的输出且没有报错，则pip成功安装。
    </p>
    <p align="center">
     <img alt="0d55dc7bd28286896b4f220465f97fbd4d7c37a3.jpg?x-bce-process=image%2Fresize%2Cm_lfit%2Cw_500%2Climit_1" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/65627907d80d31aa9842649653467839.png"/>
    </p>
    <p align="center">
     <img alt="c33acc828689a1467cb87b4cf4bd4c7c35b334a3.jpg?x-bce-process=image%2Fresize%2Cm_lfit%2Cw_500%2Climit_1" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/f42531e7474d31f1971fb228c4364922.png"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     5
    </p>
    <p>
     部分Python版本默认安装了pip，但是由于不是最新故需要升级，pip的升级命令为"python -m install pip --upgrade pip"
    </p>
    <p align="center">
     <img alt="d3c39889a146b7b122f0ea08c77c34b33d4135a3.jpg?x-bce-process=image%2Fresize%2Cm_lfit%2Cw_500%2Climit_1" src="https://i-blog.csdnimg.cn/blog_migrate/9e4ca3d193d33b49eb057712fafa12ba.png"/>
    </p>
    <p>
     END
