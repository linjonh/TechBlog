    <h2>
     <a id="mysql_0">
     </a>
     关于mysql数据库中如何存储数组以及对象类型数据
    </h2>
    <p>
     MySQL 数据库是没有专门用于存储数组和对象的数据类型的
     <br/>
     哪么如何将数组以及对象存入数据库中呢？
    </p>
    <h3>
     <a id="JSON_3">
     </a>
     JSON字符串格式
    </h3>
    <p>
     我们可以先使用 JSON.stringify() 将 数组/对象 转换为JSON字符串格式, 再将其存入数据库中
    </p>
    <p>
     *tips: 由于输出的数据是字符串，外面一层是有省略的引号的 *
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-javascript"><span class="token comment">// 1.数组</span>
<span class="token keyword">let</span> arr <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token punctuation">[</span><span class="token string">'aaa'</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span><span class="token string">'bbb'</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span><span class="token string">'ccc'</span><span class="token punctuation">]</span>
console<span class="token punctuation">.</span><span class="token function">log</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token constant">JSON</span><span class="token punctuation">.</span><span class="token function">stringify</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>arr<span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
<span class="token comment">// ["aaa","bbb","ccc"]</span>
<span class="token comment">// 2.对象</span>
<span class="token keyword">let</span> arr <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token punctuation">{<!-- --></span>
    <span class="token literal-property property">a</span><span class="token operator">:</span><span class="token string">'aaa'</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span>
    <span class="token literal-property property">b</span><span class="token operator">:</span><span class="token string">'bbb'</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span>
    <span class="token literal-property property">c</span><span class="token operator">:</span><span class="token string">'ccc'</span>
  <span class="token punctuation">}</span>
console<span class="token punctuation">.</span><span class="token function">log</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token constant">JSON</span><span class="token punctuation">.</span><span class="token function">stringify</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>arr<span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
<span class="token comment">// {"a":"aaa","b":"bbb","c":"ccc"}</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     也可以直接使用 JSON字符串的格式 存入数据库中
    </p>
    <p>
     [“aaa”,“bbb”,“ccc”]
    </p>
    <p>
     使用时再用 JSON.parse() 将 JSON字符串 转换为数组
     <br/>
     可以使用 typeof 检测数据类型
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-javascript"><span class="token comment">// 1.数组</span>
