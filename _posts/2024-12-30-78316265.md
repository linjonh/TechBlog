    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      【背景】
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     目前正在部门内部推动运维管理，
     <span style="color:#FF0000">
      无论自上而下还是自下而上，整个团队必须统一认识、达成一致目标。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     项目的成功有来自
     <span style="color:#FF0000">
      高层的支持固然事半功倍，但是更重要的是实际使用者对系统、工具、流程、思想持续不断的改进优化。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     先从思想理念开始启发，让大家思考起来，整个ppt分，运维现状、解决办法、如何实现 。
    </p>
    <p>
     核心是整理数据，流程控制，发掘能力，输出服务。
     <br/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      一、运维现状
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      1.IT运维工作忙而不受重视
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="font-family:'华文细黑'; color:#3333cc; padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:27px">
      <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
       忙
      </strong>
     </span>
     ：平台出现问题，基本都找运维处理；运维在各个业务部门间解决和处理问题，就像“救火员”、疲于奔命。即使这样运维人员的工作始终得不到认可，还经常抱怨“找不到人”、“解决问题太慢”。
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="font-family:'华文细黑'; color:#3333cc; padding:0px; margin:0px">
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:27px">
       <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
        莽
       </strong>
      </span>
     </span>
     ：苦活累活一直做，烦不了，做到后面瞎做了；有时候碍于面子，有些不符合规定的操作也做，不出事还好，出了事情只有自己默默抗；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="font-family:'华文细黑'; color:#3333cc; padding:0px; margin:0px">
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:27px">
       <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
        茫
       </strong>
      </span>
     </span>
     ：每每提笔写周报时都不知道这周瞎忙了些啥，好不容易想到一个花费较大精力的事情，却又不好意思把这点鸡毛蒜皮的事情写上去；工作难以量化。
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="font-family:'华文细黑'; color:#3333cc; padding:0px; margin:0px">
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:27px">
       <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
        盲
       </strong>
      </span>
     </span>
     ：看不到明天会发生什么，等着出问题，好一点就等着收到告警；看不到明天会改变什么，这些都是开发说了算，运维没有话语权；看不到明天自己会是什么样子，没有目标。
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="font-size:32px">
     </span>
     <span style="color:#FF0000">
      <span style="font-size:32px">
       <em>
        备注：
        <span style="font-family:'宋体'; font-size:48px; padding:0px; margin:0px">
         忙而不盲
        </span>
        <span style="font-family:Calibri; font-size:48px; padding:0px; margin:0px">
         ,
        </span>
        <span style="font-family:'宋体'; font-size:48px; padding:0px; margin:0px">
         忙而不乱。
        </span>
       </em>
      </span>
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      <br/>
     </span>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      2.IT系统复杂，维护难度高，风险压力大
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px; color:rgb(51,51,204); font-family:'华文细黑'; font-size:27px; line-height:54px">
      物
     </strong>
     ：硬软件不断增加，新技术的引入增加运维的复杂性，包括各类开发系统、各类应用架构、不同品牌厂商设备等等，需要不断增加人力投入，增加了维护难度。又有几个人能说起目前有多少资产、多少系统，它们之间的关联？
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px; color:rgb(51,51,204); font-family:'华文细黑'; font-size:27px; line-height:54px">
      人
     </strong>
     ：拿着农民工的钱，操着中南海的心；敬业些的人整天提心吊胆，求神拜佛 保佑系统与业务不挂，还有些不知者不惧，等着出问题，出现问题后反而松了口气。
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
       <br/>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
       3.内部问题
      </span>
     </span>
     <br/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
      <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px; color:rgb(51,51,204); font-family:'华文细黑'; font-size:27px; line-height:54px">
       自我认可
      </strong>
     </span>
     ：大多数时候，运维人员都在进行着机械枯燥重复的工作，且很难得到肯定；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
      <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px; color:rgb(51,51,204); font-family:'华文细黑'; font-size:27px; line-height:54px">
       流程串联
      </strong>
     </span>
     ：本是自家兄弟，沟通交流有障碍；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
      <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px; color:rgb(51,51,204); font-family:'华文细黑'; font-size:27px; line-height:54px">
       数据失准
      </strong>
     </span>
     ：实施了部分自动化，却没有真正解脱，无法判断手头信息是否真实可信，往往需要登录服务器再次确认；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
      <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px; color:rgb(51,51,204); font-family:'华文细黑'; font-size:27px; line-height:54px">
       监控失信
      </strong>
     </span>
     ：千百条告警堆积无法判断问题真正的根源，甚至重要告警被忽略。
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="font-family:'宋体'; text-indent:0in; padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:12pt">
     </span>
     <em>
      <span style="font-family:'宋体'; text-indent:0in; padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:12pt">
       PS：人员
      </span>
      <span style="font-family:'宋体'; text-indent:0in; padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:12pt">
       流动、职业前景、成就感、决策智能
      </span>
     </em>
     <br/>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin:4.32pt 0in 0pt; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; text-indent:0in; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255); vertical-align:baseline">
     <span style="font-family:Calibri; padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:12pt">
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:'宋体'; padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:12pt">
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
       二、运维管理解决办法
      </span>
     </span>
     <br/>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      1.告诉所有人，运维是如何做事的
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     运维做事也需要条件的，拒绝一句话需求；
     <br style="padding:0px; margin:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     告诉大家谁在做，做到哪一步了；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     客观的以数据说话，告诉大家结果；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <br style="padding:0px; margin:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      2.信息、状态可查可配可信任
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     资产信息、系统信息……全部统一入库；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     设备、应用、服务……被监控可告警；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     出现问题能找到处理人、故障点、操作手册……；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <br style="padding:0px; margin:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      3.建立维护中心的基础
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     从原始人的体力劳动进入使用工具的石器时代；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     根据维护中心标准和流程来优化现有数据与工具；
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      <br/>
     </span>
     三、
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
      服务意识
     </span>
    </p>
    <p>
     <span style="font-family:SimSun; color:#2c2c2c; font-size:20px; font-weight:bold; line-height:20px; text-align:center; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:20px">
      <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
       1.服务意识萌芽
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     使自身掌握的资源与能力变为服务，向内部、外部提供这种服务。
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:16px">
       梳理整合自身的资源，再结合自身的能力，制定服务的标准，定义服务的内容
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="font-size:12px; padding:0px; margin:0px">
      。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <br style="padding:0px; margin:0px"/>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:20px">
      <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
       2.何为服务
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      系统资源服务
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     1.硬件资源运算/存储/灾备等能力+技术(VM/HA等)=资源服务；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     2.用户提供:产品规划、 UV/PV 、存储/接口等需求；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     3.维护沟通评估提供:服务器(cpu、mem、disk)、带宽、ip等
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     4.周期性报告：使用前测试报告、使用后状态报告、阶段的从业务角度分析服务运行报告。
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      监控服务
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     在提供监控能力后，需要后续的反馈监控的效果与各项指标，帮助需求方不断改进监控成效。
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">

     </span>
     <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      安全服务
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">

     </span>

      查询服务
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      等等
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">

    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; font-size:20px">
      <strong style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
       3.建立服务意识
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     首先，了解自身能提供哪些能力与服务
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      服务可大可小，要显现出各自岗位中的能力与价值
     </span>
     ；
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     其次，服务需形成闭环
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px">
      服务并不是在操作完成后即结束，还需在之后统计与分析，找出其中的问题，体现服务的价值；
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
     </span>
     最后，需不断改进
    </p>
    <p style="padding:0px; margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; clear:both; height:auto; color:rgb(44,44,44); font-family:'宋体','Arial Narrow',arial,serif; font-size:14px; line-height:28px; white-space:normal; background-color:rgb(255,255,255)">
     <span style="padding:0px; margin:0px; white-space:pre">
