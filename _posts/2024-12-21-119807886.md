    <h2>
     <a id="PGor_16">
     </a>
     一、如何在PG数据库中根据字段or注释查询所在的表和字段名称？
    </h2>
    <p>
     <font color="#999AAA">
      相信很多的小伙伴们跟我一样，初入职场，需要经历时间的过渡，在学校或者培训机构，基本上使用的都是mySql数据库，或者有学习大数据的同学说自己用的是Oracle数据库，这些都是我们在工作中对数据操作的工具，在工作之后，会因为不同的公司的需求不同，所以使用数据库一样，就像我之前使用的是mySQL,但现在的工作就要使用PG数据库，首先要做的就是适应语句之间的不同。因为很多时候，告诉你用哪张表里的哪个字段，都会去找，但是在整理数据的时候，可能你只有一个字段名称或者只有一个注释，这个时候就需要用到下面的语句了，仅供参考吧。
     </font>
    </p>
    <h3>
     <a id="1_21">
     </a>
     1.根据字段名称查询所在表
    </h3>
    <p>
     <font color="#999AAA">
      语句如下：
     </font>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-c">SELECT
a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attname as <span class="token string">"字段名"</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span>
c<span class="token punctuation">.</span>relname as <span class="token string">"表名"</span>
FROM
pg_class as c<span class="token punctuation">,</span>
pg_attribute as a<span class="token punctuation">,</span>
pg_type as t
WHERE
a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attname <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token string">'XXXX'</span>
and a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>atttypid <span class="token operator">=</span> t<span class="token punctuation">.</span>oid
and a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attrelid <span class="token operator">=</span> c<span class="token punctuation">.</span>oid
and a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attnum<span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token number">0</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>
</code></pre>
    <p>
     使用的时候只需要把’XXX’改成你所要查询的字段就可以了。
    </p>
    <h3>
     <a id="2_44">
     </a>
     2.根据字段名称查询注释
    </h3>
    <p>
     <font color="#999AAA">
      语句如下：
     </font>
    </p>
    <pre><code class="prism language-c">SELECT
a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attname as <span class="token string">"字段名"</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span>
<span class="token function">col_description</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attrelid<span class="token punctuation">,</span>a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attnum<span class="token punctuation">)</span> as <span class="token string">"注释"</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span>
<span class="token function">concat_ws</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token string">''</span><span class="token punctuation">,</span>t<span class="token punctuation">.</span>typname<span class="token punctuation">,</span><span class="token function">SUBSTRING</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span><span class="token function">format_type</span><span class="token punctuation">(</span>a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>atttypid<span class="token punctuation">,</span>a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>atttypmod<span class="token punctuation">)</span> from <span class="token string">'.∗.∗'</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span><span class="token punctuation">)</span> as <span class="token string">"字段类型"</span>
FROM
pg_class as c<span class="token punctuation">,</span>
pg_attribute as a<span class="token punctuation">,</span>
pg_type as t
WHERE
a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attname <span class="token operator">=</span> <span class="token string">'XXXX'</span>
and a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>atttypid <span class="token operator">=</span> t<span class="token punctuation">.</span>oid
and a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attrelid <span class="token operator">=</span> c<span class="token punctuation">.</span>oid
and a<span class="token punctuation">.</span>attnum<span class="token operator">&gt;</span><span class="token number">0</span><span class="token punctuation">;</span>

</code></pre>
<p>
<font color="#999AAA">
同理修改’XXX’即可。
</font>
</p>
