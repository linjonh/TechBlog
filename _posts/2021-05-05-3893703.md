     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">

     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">

     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      从空间数据的采集、编辑到数据的处理分析及结果输出
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">

     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      然后选用某种程序设计语言在一定操作系统平台上编程实现。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 150%; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;">
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
      </span>

    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">

      <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">

      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 150%; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;">
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      嵌入式
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">

     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">

     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      软件由于其平台的特殊性
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      使得嵌入式
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      软件
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">

      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      的特征与普通
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       PC
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      上的
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      软件相比有着明显不同
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      如下表：
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 150%; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;">
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 150%; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;">
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      由于嵌入式
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      平台的特殊性
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      在进行嵌入式
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      开发设计时需要遵循以下几点原则：
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 150%; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;">
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
       <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
        4.1
       </span>
      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
       尽量减少移动端数据本地存储量
      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 150%; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;">
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      嵌入式设备通常只有有限的存储空间
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      虽然技术的不断提高使存储器的容量不断增加
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      但在大多数应用中
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      存储容量仍然是宝贵的。所以嵌入式
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      系统必须是低存储要求的。从服务器端获取所需数据是一个非常好的办法。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
       <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
        4.2
       </span>
      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
       合理组织空间数据的逻辑结构
      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      鉴于嵌入式
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      的开发平台和移动设备性能的考虑
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      为了提高软件的运行速度和节约设备的内存空间
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      嵌入式
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      软件应该具有自身的空间数据模型及适应该数据模型的数据结构。具体来说，对于图形数据而言，尽量用整形坐标数据来代替双精度型或浮点型数据，以便来节约存储单元。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
       <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
        4.3
       </span>
      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">

      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
       <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
        GIS
       </span>
      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
       数据
      </span>
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      通常整幅图的
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      数据量是庞大的，在嵌入式
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       GIS
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      中，我们只考虑当前需要研究的地图数据及图层，其它不予考虑，这样就可以避免不必要的内存开销。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; line-height: 150%;">
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
       <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
        4.4
       </span>
      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
      <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
       合理设计用户界面
      </span>
     </strong>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal;">
     </strong>
    </p>
    <p style="margin: 0cm 0cm 0pt; text-indent: 21pt; line-height: 150%; mso-char-indent-count: 2.0;">
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      移动设备的屏幕不仅分辨率低而且形状也各不相同
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       ,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      如同是采用
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       Windows Mobile
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      的
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       Pocket PC
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      屏幕分辨率为
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       320X240,
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      而
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       Smart Phone
      </span>
     </span>
     <span style="line-height: 150%; font-family: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      则为
     </span>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="line-height: 150%; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt;">
      <span style="font-family: Times New Roman;">
       176 X 220,
