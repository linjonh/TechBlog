      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; border:0px; outline:0px; font-size:18px; vertical-align:baseline; background:transparent">

      </strong>
     </span>

     </span>

       </span>
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style='margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:22px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; font-family:"Microsoft YaHei"; background:transparent; border:0px; outline:0px; font-size:15px; vertical-align:baseline; color:rgb(102,102,102); line-height:26px'>

        <br/>

      </strong>
     </span>

     <span style="background-color:transparent; font-size:16px">
      <strong>
       二、数据库快照
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style='margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:22px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; font-family:"Microsoft YaHei"; background:transparent; border:0px; outline:0px; font-size:15px; vertical-align:baseline; color:rgb(102,102,102); line-height:26px'>
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; border:0px; outline:0px; font-size:18px; vertical-align:baseline; color:black">
      <strong>
       数据库快照是数据库（源数据库）的只读、静态视图。多个快照可以位于一个源数据库中，并且可以作为数据库始终驻留在同一服务器实例上。创建快照时，每个数据库快照在事务上与源数据库一致。在被数据库所有者显式删除之前，快照始终存在。
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style='margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:22px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; font-family:"Microsoft YaHei"; background:transparent; border:0px; outline:0px; font-size:15px; vertical-align:baseline; color:rgb(102,102,102); line-height:26px'>

     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; border:0px; outline:0px; font-size:18px; vertical-align:baseline; background:transparent">

      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style='margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:22px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; font-family:"Microsoft YaHei"; background:transparent; border:0px; outline:0px; font-size:15px; vertical-align:baseline; color:rgb(102,102,102); line-height:26px'>
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; border:0px; outline:0px; font-size:18px; vertical-align:baseline; background:transparent">
      <strong>
       2、数据库对象；系统只有系统表，系统视图。自定义有包含系统的，同时有自定义的表跟视图。
      </strong>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style='margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:22px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; font-family:"Microsoft YaHei"; background:transparent; border:0px; outline:0px; font-size:15px; vertical-align:baseline; color:rgb(102,102,102); line-height:26px'>
