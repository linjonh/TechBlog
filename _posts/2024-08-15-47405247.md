     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">

     </span>

     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">

     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      我们讲讨论当前的痛点以及如何用更好的方式应用大数据。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      大数据为企业提供了一个更好的提高生产力和收入的机会。然而
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      企业在大数据收集上就遇到了麻烦。
     </span>
     2012
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      年，通过对
     </span>
     300
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      位高管和经理们的调查，清楚的展示了企业在管理大数据过程中的挑战和困难。下面是调查的要点
     </span>
     :
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     66%
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      的受访者希望企业里可以有更多的人使用分析工具。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     59%
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      的受访者认为
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      现有的分析框架处理大数据太慢了。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     57%
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      的受访者认为他们的分析框架无法与大数据的流入网络的速度匹配。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     55%
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      的受访者认为他们的分析工具也难以使用
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      因为他们不能提供易于理解的信息格式。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      上述结果非常好的总结了与大数据企业面临的挑战。然而
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      自
     </span>
     2012
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      年以来
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      问题变得更加复杂
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      原因是数据量一直在以惊人的速度增加，同时企业对大数据管理的态度也出现问题。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      虽然面对技术、硬件和软件方面的挑战
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      但企业应对大数据还是不够谨慎，这似乎是根本问题。下面的说明大数据的主要痛点：
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; color:rgb(0,176,80); word-wrap:break-word!important">
       错误对待大数据
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      大数据管理策略实际上反映了营销人员如何看待大数据及其潜在价值。到目前为止
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      大数据管理缺乏严肃性和一致性。据《哈佛商业评论》的一篇文章中，尽管大数据被公众炒的很热，企业中营销人员依赖于大数据还非常有限，只有
     </span>
     11%
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      的客户相关决定是通过大数据决策的。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      显然
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      大家对大数据言行不一致。《哈佛商业评论》
     </span>
     (Harvard Business Review)
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      调查发现
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">

     </span>
     60
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      岁以上的人，你可能不用考虑有一个
     </span>
     Facebook
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      页面
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      因为根据你的经验
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      年纪大的人一般不使用
     </span>
     Facebook
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      。但在一个复杂而变化的商业环境中
     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      这种假设可能就是错的。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; white-space:pre-wrap; color:rgb(68,68,68); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:13px; line-height:18.2000007629395px; text-indent:28px; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">

     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">

     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">

     </span>
     ,
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:宋体; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      因为他们坚持不断重置哪些不需要校正的目标。
     </span>
    </p>

     </span>
