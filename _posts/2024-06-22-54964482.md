     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; white-space:pre-wrap">
      1、视频编码标准两大系统是什么？
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; color:rgb(62,62,62); font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">

    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      视频编码标准有两大系统：MPEG和ITU-T，如下
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; color:rgb(62,62,62); font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      视频编码标准
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; color:rgb(62,62,62); font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      MPEG标准由MPEG制定
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; color:rgb(62,62,62); font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">

      MPEG-1 | MPEG-2 | (MPEG-3) | MPEG-4 | MPEG-7 | MPEG-21
     </span>

      H.261 | (H.262) | H.263 | H.263v2 | H.264

    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; color:rgb(62,62,62); font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">

     2、什么是音视频编码格式？什么是音视频封装格式？
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      常见的AVI、RMVB、MKV、ASF、WMV、MP4、3GP、FLV等文件其实只能算是一种封装标准。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      一个完整的视频文件是由音频和视频2部分组成的。H264、Xvid等就是视频编码格式，MP3、AAC等就是音频编码格式。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      例如：将一个Xvid视频编码文件和一个MP3视频编码文件按AVI封装标准封装以后，就得到一个AVI后缀的视频文件，这个就是我们常见的AVI视频文件了。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      由于很多种视频编码文件、音频编码文件都符合AVI封装要求，则意味着即使是AVI后缀，也可能里面的具体编码格式不同。因此出现在一些设备上，同是AVI后缀文件，一些能正常播放，还有一些就无法播放。
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; color:rgb(255,0,0); word-wrap:break-word!important">
       同样的情况也存在于其他容器格式。即使RMVB、WMV等也不例外，
      </span>
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; color:rgb(255,0,0); word-wrap:break-word!important">
       事实上，很多封装容器对音频编码和视频编码的组合方式放的很开，如AVI还可以使用H.264+AAC组合，可以在具体使用中自己体会。尤其是MKV封装容器，基本无论什么样的组合都可以！但一般MKV用的最多的就是H.264+AAC组合，此组合文件体积最小，清晰度最高。因此网上很多MKV视频都是高清晰度的。
      </span>
      <br/>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; color:rgb(255,0,0); word-wrap:break-word!important">
       <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
        因此，视频转换需要设置的本质就是：A设置需要的视频编码、B设置需要的音频编码、C选择需要的容器封装。一个完整的视频转换设置都至少包括了上面3个步骤。
       </span>
       <br/>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; color:rgb(255,0,0); word-wrap:break-word!important">
       <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
        <span style="color:rgb(47,47,47); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
         3、平时说的软解和硬解，具体是什么？
        </span>
        <br/>
       </span>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; color:rgb(255,0,0); word-wrap:break-word!important">
       <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
        <span style="color:rgb(47,47,47); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
        </span>
       </span>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; color:rgb(62,62,62); font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      硬解就是硬件解码，指利用GPU来部分代替CPU进行解码，软解就是软件解码，指利用软件让CPU来进行解码。两者的具体区别如下所示：
     </span>
    </p>
    <div>
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      <br/>
     </span>
    </div>
    <br/>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; color:rgb(255,0,0); word-wrap:break-word!important">
       <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
        <span style="color:rgb(47,47,47); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
         <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
          硬解码：是将原来全部交由CPU来处理的视频数据的一部分交由GPU来做，而GPU的并行运算能力要远远高于CPU，这样可以大大的降低对CPU的负载，CPU的占用率较低了之后就可以同时运行一些其他的程序了，当然，对于较好的处理器来说，比如i5 2320，或者AMD 任何一款四核心处理器来说，硬解和软件的区别只是个人偏好问题了吧。
         </span>
         <br/>
        </span>
       </span>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; color:rgb(255,0,0); word-wrap:break-word!important">
       <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
        <span style="color:rgb(47,47,47); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
         <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
          <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
           软解码：即通过软件让CPU来对视频进行解码处理；而硬解码：指不借助于CPU，而通过专用的子卡设备来独立完成视频解码任务。曾经的VCD/DVD解压卡、视频压缩卡等都隶属于硬解码这个范畴。而现如今，要完成高清解码已经不再需要额外的子卡，因为硬解码的模块已经被整合到显卡GPU的内部，所以目前的主流显卡（集显）都能够支持硬解码技术。
          </span>
          <br/>
         </span>
        </span>
       </span>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; color:rgb(255,0,0); word-wrap:break-word!important">

           <span style="color:rgb(47,47,47); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; white-space:pre-wrap">
            4、何为直播？何为点播？
           </span>
           <br/>
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </span>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:25px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; font-size:16px; font-family:微软雅黑; word-break:break-word; color:rgb(47,47,47); line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
     <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
      <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; color:rgb(255,0,0); word-wrap:break-word!important">
       <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
        <span style="color:rgb(47,47,47); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
         <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
          <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
           <span style="color:rgb(47,47,47); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px; white-space:pre-wrap">
            <span style="color:rgb(255,0,0); font-family:微软雅黑; font-size:16px">
             直播：是一个三方交互(主播、服务器、观众)，这个交互式实时的！尽管会根据选择的协议不同而有一些延迟，但我们仍认为它直播是实时的！---&gt;主播在本地发送音视频给服务器（推流），观众从服务器实时解码（拉流）收看收听主播发送给服务器的音视频（直播内容）。直播是不能快进的
            </span>
            <br/>
           </span>
          </span>
         </span>
        </span>
       </span>
      </span>
     </span>
    </p>

                <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important">

       <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">

       </span>
       <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0); white-space:pre-wrap">
        RTMP协议比较全能，既可以用来推送又可以用来直播，其核心理念是将大块的视频帧和音频帧“剁碎”，然后以小数据包的形式在互联网上进行传输，而且支持加密，因此隐私性相对比较理想，但拆包组包的过程比较复杂，所以在海量并发时也容易出现一些不可预期的稳定性问题。
       </span>

       <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">

       </span>
      </p>
     </li>
     <li style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important">
      <p style="margin-top:0px; margin-bottom:0px; padding-top:0px; padding-bottom:0px; max-width:100%; clear:both; min-height:1em; line-height:1.75em; word-wrap:break-word!important">
       <span style="margin:0px; padding:0px; max-width:100%; word-wrap:break-word!important; color:rgb(255,0,0)">
        3、HLS：苹果原生：HTTP Live Streaming，遵循的是 HTTP 超文本传输协议，端口号8080，将视频分成5-10秒的视频小分片，然后用m3u8索引表进行管理，由于客户端下载到的视频都是5-10秒的完整数据，故视频的流畅性很好，但也同样引入了很大的延迟（HLS的一般延迟在10-30s左右）。
       </span>
      </p>
