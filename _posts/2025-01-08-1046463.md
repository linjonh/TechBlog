    <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt">
     <font size="3">
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       嵌入式系统的核心部件是各种类型的嵌入式处理器，目前据不完全统计，全世界嵌入式处理器的品种总量已经超过
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        1000
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       多种，流行体系结构有
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        30
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       几个系列，其中
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        8051
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       体系的占有多半。生产
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        8051
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       单片机的半导体厂家有
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        20
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       多个，共
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        350
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       多种衍生产品，仅
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        Philips
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       就有近
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        100
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       种。现在几乎每个半导体制造商都生产嵌入式处理器，越来越多的公司有自己的处理器设计部门。嵌入式处理器的寻址空间一般从
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        64kB
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       到
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        16MB
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       ，处理速度从
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        0.1 MIPS
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       到
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        2000 MIPS
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       ，常用封装从
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        8
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       个引脚到
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        144
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       个引脚。根据其现状，嵌入式计算机可以分成下面几类。
      </span>
     </font>
     <font size="3">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        <br/>
        <br/>
       </font>
      </span>
      <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal">
       <span style="COLOR: blue; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
        嵌入式微处理器
       </span>
      </strong>
      <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal">
       <span lang="EN-US" style="COLOR: blue; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
        <font face="Times New Roman">
         (Embedded Microprocessor Unit, EMPU)
        </font>
       </span>
      </strong>
     </font>
     <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <br/>
      </span>
     </strong>
     <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
      <br/>
     </span>
     <font size="3">
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       嵌入式微处理器的基础是通用计算机中的
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        CPU
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       。在应用中，将微处理器装配在专门设计的电路板上，只保留和嵌入式应用有关的母板功能，这样可以大幅度减小系统体积和功耗。为了满足嵌入式应用的特殊要求，嵌入式微处理器虽然在功能上和标准微处理器基本是一样的，但在工作温度、抗电磁干扰、可靠性等方面一般都做了各种增强。
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
      </span>
     </font>
    </p>
    <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt">
     <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
      <font face="Times New Roman" size="3">
      </font>
     </span>
    </p>
    <p class="MsoNormal" style="MARGIN: 0cm 0cm 0pt">
     <font size="3">
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       和工业控制计算机相比，嵌入式微处理器具有体积小、重量轻、成本低、可靠性高的优点，但是在电路板上必须包括
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        ROM
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        RAM
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、总线接口、各种外设等器件，从而降低了系统的可靠性，技术保密性也较差。嵌入式微处理器及其存储器、总线、外设等安装在一块电路板上，称为单板计算机。如
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        STD-BUS
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        PC104
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       等。近年来，德国、日本的一些公司又开发出了类似
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        “
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       火柴盒
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        ”
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       式名片大小的嵌入式计算机系列
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        OEM
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       产品。
      </span>
     </font>
     <font size="3">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        <br/>
        <br/>
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       嵌入式处理器目前主要有
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        Am186/88
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        386EX
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        SC-400
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        Power PC
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        68000
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        MIPS
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        ARM
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       系列等。
      </span>
     </font>
     <font size="3">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        <br/>
        <br/>
       </font>
      </span>
      <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal">
       <span style="COLOR: blue; FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
        嵌入式微控制器
       </span>
      </strong>
      <strong style="mso-bidi-font-weight: normal">
       <span lang="EN-US" style="COLOR: blue; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
        <font face="Times New Roman">
         (Microcontroller Unit, MCU)
        </font>
       </span>
      </strong>
     </font>
     <font size="3">
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        <br/>
        <br/>
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       嵌入式微控制器又称单片机，顾名思义，就是将整个计算机系统集成到一块芯片中。嵌入式微控制器一般以某一种微处理器内核为核心，芯片内部集成
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        ROM/EPROM
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        RAM
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、总线、总线逻辑、定时
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        /
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       计数器、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        WatchDog
       </font>
      </span>
      <span style="FONT-FAMILY: 宋体; mso-ascii-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-hansi-font-family: 'Times New Roman'; mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       、
      </span>
      <span lang="EN-US" style="mso-bidi-font-size: 10.5pt">
       <font face="Times New Roman">
        I/O
